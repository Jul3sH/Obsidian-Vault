# Artifact — Julian's Email to Dad & Wendy, 29 July 2023 ("Being Kept Informed")

> **Type:** Primary artifact — full verbatim email. Sent by Julian to a joint mailbox for Felix and Wendy. Preserved for legal/evidential reference. Cross-referenced from [[dad-finance-discussions]] and [[dad-health-discussions]].
>
> **Why it matters:** This is Julian's clearest contemporaneous, documented statement that he (a) actively sought to be involved in Dad's medical care, (b) was rebuffed, and (c) cares deeply about Dad's wellbeing. It directly rebuts the narrative — which Wendy has repeatedly advanced — that Julian is an uninvolved/uncaring son. That narrative matters to Wendy because positioning herself as **sole carer** strengthens her hand against any contest over inheritance. See "Significance" below.

## Source
- **From:** Julian Hart <julianhart@gmail.com>
- **Sent:** Sat, 29 Jul 2023, 11:05
- **To:** Felix / Wendy (joint mailbox — addressed to "Dear Dad and Wendy")

## Verbatim text

> Dear Dad and Wendy,
>
> In response to accusations I'm not concerned about Dad's medical condition, as well as being advised neither of you are obligated to keep me informed about it, I would like to remind you of several facts that explain why I feel confused, frustrated and hurt with the situation.
>
> When Dad explained he had a medical condition I requested to accompany him on physician trips as he had done to me through my ill health. I was told that Wendy wanted to be the one to do this and respected the request despite being very disappointed.
>
> What I asked for in return was to be kept informed with regards to the prognosis and to be updated after any developments. I asked again on the WhatSapp call when the three of us were discussing Dad's will, when we all met at the USRC, and on numerous phone calls with Dad.
>
> So based on previous agreements there is an obligation to keep me informed which has not been upheld, and subsequently it seems very reasonable that I call this out. To date I have not received a single formal diagnosis, I have not been updated after medical trips, and when I do get updates they are very vague. Even the recent update is incomplete when I compare it to the information I got from Hil.
>
> I initially let this go because I thought it was Dad being stoic as Father to Son, as he has always been in ill health, and wanted to respect this. In addition to this I was being told that there was no formal diagnosis. It now appears there is a formal diagnosis and medical plan which I have never been clearly informed about, so it's unsurpising that I am feeling excluded.
>
> The messages from you both suggest that updates are conditional on me calling you and also that I do not care about Dad's condition because I am not making regular calls to you both. I had better address this narrative as it is misleading and hurtful.
>
> Wendy, on 25th June you responded to my request that the three of us meet stating that you have a deaf ear and would get confused having a conversation. I suspected this to be unlikely having spoken to you face to face days earlier and took it as an indication I should communicate by text which is what I subsequently did.
>
> Dad, with regards to calling you the sad truth of the matter is that I can't recall the last time you answered. I have tried calling, video calling with Sophia, and as a recent example I couldn't get through when I was calling to tell you news of my own diagnosis. Your phone regularly shows you have not accessed it for the entire duration of the day.
>
> As things are now developing my request is simply that I am kept informed. Considering a formal diagnosis is now available I would like to be told what it is and what the medical plan is.
>
> Dad, perhaps we can agree on a couple of regular slots to call each week and you are always welcome to call me when you have your phone to hand.
>
> I hope this email is well received as my explanation of recent events, reiterating that I care very much about your wellbeing Dad. I have cared about your wellbeing for my 50+ years of conscious life and we will never fall out to a degree where that changes. Please do remember this.
>
> Love Julian

## Significance (for the contribution-defence and inheritance context)

- **Rebuts the "uncaring/uninvolved son" narrative.** Julian *asked* to accompany Dad to medical appointments and was told Wendy wanted that role; he then asked only to be kept informed. The lack of involvement was **imposed, not chosen**.
- **Documents a prior agreement to keep Julian informed** (WhatsApp will call, the USRC meeting, numerous phone calls) — which was then not upheld.
- **Documents the access problem from Julian's side:** Dad rarely answered; Wendy deflected the three-way meeting citing a "deaf ear"; Julian couldn't even reach Dad to share his own cancer diagnosis.
- **Closes with an unambiguous statement of love and care** ("I care very much about your wellbeing… for my 50+ years… we will never fall out to a degree where that changes").

**Julian's read of the dynamic:** Wendy reacted very defensively to this email ("I'm under no obligation whatsoever to keep you informed"), which Julian believes hit a nerve — because being seen as **sole carer** strengthens her position against any contest over Dad's estate, and Julian documenting his attempts to be involved undercuts that. The same logic explains the 3 June 2026 request that Julian "help with walking" (with a cost mention): framing Julian as unwilling to support Dad reinforces her sole-carer narrative. **[Opinion]**

**Immediate aftermath — video-call legal threat (30 July 2023):** The day after this email, on a FaceTime/video call, Felix said words to the effect of *"Son, if you carry on like this, we'll have to take legal action against you,"* referring to the email exchange. Julian replied *"What are you talking about, Dad? I'm just keen to understand how you're doing."* Julian's account: Wendy was orchestrating this in the background while Dad — by then cognitively impaired — was effectively being puppeteered. **Date confirmed** by Julian's 31 Jul 2023 WhatsApp ("taken aback by your response *yesterday*… you threatened me with lawyers") → the call was 30 Jul 2023; also referenced Jan 2026 ("my own father got on a video call, with you in the background, and threatened to take me to court for asking that I was kept informed"). **[Recollection, corroborated by WhatsApp references]**
