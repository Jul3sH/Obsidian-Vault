## Vision

> *Be a devoted father to Sophia above all else, surrounded by loyal and loving relationships — with Mum, a life partner, and friends — that bring joy, not exhaustion.*

---

## Principles

Hard rules that apply to every Relationships decision, regardless of circumstance. Must always adhere — these are the "am I allowed to proceed?" check.

1. **Sophia First** — Sophia's wellbeing and future override every other relationship decision. Non-negotiable.
2. **Never Enter a Financially Unequal Partnership** — Any life partner must be financially independent or at minimum not a financial liability. No one uses this relationship as a route to my money or leaves me worse off than I started. This principle exists to protect against emotional override — attraction alone cannot carry you past this gate.
3. **Cut Toxic Relationships** — Bad relationships are mentally exhausting and actively harmful. Eliminate them; do not manage or tolerate them.
4. **No Guilt-Driven Compromise — Clodagh** — Clodagh has had many chances and has repeatedly taken without reciprocating. Her unresolved issues, including alcohol addiction, are not mine to absorb. I will not compromise my lifestyle or Sophia's stability out of guilt for choices she has not made. Supporting her is a choice made from genuine care; being held hostage by guilt is not acceptable.
5. **Speak to Mum Every Day** — Contact Mum at least once every single day without exception. Does not have to be face-to-face — a call or message is enough. Non-negotiable.

---

## Values

What I care about in relationships — the "do I want this?" check. Directional guides that shape decisions.

1. **Mum — Purpose, Friendship, and Love** — Be her best friend and give her a reason to get up in the morning. Make sure she knows she is loved and that I care about her, while she is still here to hear it.
2. **Fun Partners** — Make the most of the fact that I'm in good shape, reasonably well off, and still young enough to have fun before committing to a life partner.
3. **Life Partner** — Only enter a life partnership that is genuinely fulfilling: real attraction, shared interests, loyalty, fun, no drama. Don't settle, don't stay out of obligation.
4. **Clodagh — Friendship and Recovery** — Value her as a friend and continue to invest in that friendship. Care deeply about her recovery from addiction — for her own sake, and so that she and Sophia can have a real mother-daughter relationship. She deserves to get back to who she is.
5. **Invest in Good Relationships** — Friendships are important and historically neglected. As I get older, I realise how much they matter. Invest time in them.

## 10-year Ambitions

> *Where I want to be by 2036. Directional and aspirational — not strictly measured, but each should have a loose sense of "how would I know I'm on track?"*

- **Sophia Is Set Up for Life** — By 2036 Sophia has graduated from a good university with a strong degree, and I have given her a meaningful head start: a contribution toward a property deposit, or an inheritance that gives her a foundation I never had. She will never experience what I went through — someone coming in and eroding my ability to pass something on to her. Whatever it takes, that protection is in place and the asset is preserved for her.
- **Mum Is Cared For and Not Alone** — Mum is well looked after, feels safe, and knows she is not isolated. Whether I am in the UK full-time or travelling from abroad, I am present enough in her life that she never feels abandoned. The financial freedom to get to her quickly — or to be based nearby — is in place before she needs it most. She does not spend her final years feeling scared or on her own.
- **Life Partner** — Settled in a committed relationship with a woman I find genuinely attractive even as she gets older, who I have a shared interests with so we don't get bored, who is loyal, trustworthy and ideally financially independent so I don't risk Sophia's inheritance. In terms of age I need to find the right balance between her looking attractive, but not being so young that I look old and she leaves me.

## 5-year Directions

> *Where I need to be by 2031 to be on track for the 10-year Ambitions. More grounded — aligned to the Ambitions above.*

- **Sophia — Environment for Success and Enjoyment** — Sophia is in an environment that breeds both success and happiness. She is meeting her full potential while genuinely enjoying her childhood and adolescence. When she looks back on growing up, the memories are good ones.
- **Mum — Cared For and Not Alone** — Mum is well looked after, feels safe, and knows she is not isolated. Present enough in her life that she never feels abandoned. The financial freedom to get to her quickly — or to be based nearby — is in place before she needs it most.
- **Fun Partners** — Attractive, fun, sexually compatible partners to enjoy the next 5 years with while I am at my most appealing. A good sexual relationship is a priority. Date women who also have the traits of a 'safe' long-term partner so that the option to transition exists. Any long-term partner chosen at this stage will need to get on with Sophia, who will still be living with me.
