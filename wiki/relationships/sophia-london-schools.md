### Ursuline — Catholic admissions
**Julian's status (7 Jul):** married in a Catholic church; registered with a Catholic parish in Wimbledon; Catholic wedding. **BUT** no evidence of *practising* as Catholics in HK (no parish that can confirm ongoing Mass attendance). Faith-priority categories in Catholic school policies typically require a priest's *Certificate of Catholic Practice* (regular Mass attendance) — which he likely **cannot** evidence. Realistic read: he'd fall into a **lower-priority category** (baptised-not-practising / non-Catholic), NOT top faith priority. **However, faith ranking may be moot for in-year entry if a genuine vacancy exists** (schools often take whoever applies when there's a free place and no waiting list). Confirm both points with the school directly.

Catholic practice and evidence matter for admissions ranking under the Ursuline policy. The exact attendance rule (e.g. weekly Mass for X years) is not confirmed here — **do not assume a specific requirement; read the full admissions policy before applying.** Policy: [ursulinehigh.merton.sch.uk/assets/2025-2026/Admissions/Admissions-Policy-2026.pdf](https://www.ursulinehigh.merton.sch.uk/assets/2025-2026/Admissions/Admissions-Policy-2026.pdf)
