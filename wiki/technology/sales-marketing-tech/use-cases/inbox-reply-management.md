#Unified Inbox & Reply Management

**Job to be done:** Manage all replies from multiple sending accounts in one place, classify intent, and handle responses efficiently.

## Inputs
- Replies arriving across multiple sending mailboxes
- Full email thread context
- Prospect record and campaign history

## Outputs
- Unified view of all replies across all accounts
- AI classification labels (interested, OOO, not interested, objection, booking intent)
- Drafted or sent responses
- Hot lead notifications to team / Slack
- CRM updates from replies

## Key Capabilities to Evaluate
- **Unified inbox** — consolidates 10+ mailboxes into a single view
- **Cross-channel consolidation** — email + LinkedIn replies in one place
- **AI reply classification** — auto-tags intent without manual reading
- **Thread-aware classification** — reads full thread, not just last email
- **Intent detection** — identifies hot leads, booking signals, objections
- **AI reply drafting** — writes responses based on thread context and your tone
- **Auto-reply (Autopilot mode)** — sends without human approval; configurable per category
- **Human-in-the-loop** — AI drafts, human approves before send
- **Team routing** — assigns hot leads to specific SDRs with Slack/email notifications
- **Objection handling** — AI follows a playbook to respond to common objections
- **CRM sync from replies** — auto-creates or updates deals on positive replies

## Vendors in This Category
Snov.io, Apollo, HubSpot, Instantly, Lemlist, Saleshandy, Clay, n8n, Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, Hermes Agent, OpenClaw

## Key Takeaways
- Instantly's Unibox with AI Reply Agent is the most complete native solution — autopilot, booking detection, Slack routing
- OpenClaw's TriageEngine handles 80–90% of replies automatically with YAML-configured rules and dry-run safety mode
- Hermes Agent maintains full thread context across all channels (email, Slack, Telegram, WhatsApp) with persistent memory
- Claude Code + Gmail MCP can classify and draft replies but requires configuration — not plug-and-play
- HubSpot's CRM-native inbox is strongest for teams already using HubSpot as their CRM
