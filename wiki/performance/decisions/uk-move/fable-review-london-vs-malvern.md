# Fable adversarial review - London vs Malvern (7 Jul 2026)

> Hostile review of the London-direct recommendation. Briefed to hunt motivated reasoning (Julian admitted London is what he *wants*), steelman Malvern-first, justify/reject the flip from Fable's 4-Jul S3-primary verdict, and stress the numbers + FIG claim. Companion to the primary-decision [[fable-review|4-Jul Fable review]].

## Verdict: SURVIVES WITH CONDITIONS

London-direct is the right call - but leaner-funded, harder-executed, and more honestly argued than the paperwork claimed. It survives because its two load-bearing legs hold under attack: **(1) Sophia's single-vs-double disruption**, and **(2) the Malvern plan defeats its own goal** (any real job is London-hybrid → crash-pad → Sophia orphaned midweek; a village AWS retrain weakens his existing BFSI edge). The money leg survives **degraded**: ~4 years honest runway, not ~5.

## Motivated-reasoning audit (what was real vs flattered)
- **Money / runway** - REAL directionally, **INFLATED numerically.** London living (£2,162/mo) was 40-60% light vs HK actuals. Honest: ~£4,400/mo burn, ~4yr runway, first year £55-70k. Still clears a long search. *(Model §9-10 re-baselined.)*
- **FIG tax exemption** - REAL but **not load-bearing**; the anchor doc had laundered the unconfirmed 10-year condition into "fear neutralised." Worst case ~£2,900/yr, location-neutral. *(why-london.md fixed.)*
- **Age-timing** - PARTIALLY RATIONALISED (used age as urgency-for-London, never as pessimism-about-London). Honest version still favours London: search may be long *because* of age → need the runway and to start now, not at 56.
- **Network** - REAL but UNTESTED (a decade-dormant network is an assertion until 20 messages are sent). London ≫ Malvern's zero, but don't book it as an asset yet.
- **Sophia's preference** - REAL but THIN (a teenager, one friend, relayed partly via drunk Clodagh; she's never had Malvern properly put to her). What actually carries it: single-vs-double disruption + the crash-pad/promise-breach, not her vote.
- **"Buffer defeats its own goal"** - REAL. The best analysis in the file. Caveat: in a 2+ year search-failure tail, Malvern *does* preserve ~£29k/yr - but the floor is never threatened either way, so it's loss-aversion, not ruin-risk.
- **Structural tell:** the anchor's "asymmetry box" rigged the comparison (London vs *open-ended* Malvern - a strawman). London wins the honest comparison anyway. *(Fixed.)*

1. **Sophia's exact school year is UNKNOWN** while the whole decision is anchored on her Sept start. If she's entering **Year 10 (GCSE)**, Malvern-first is near-disqualified (mid-GCSE move) - decisive for London - but London school-place execution becomes critical.
2. **London in-year school admission is nowhere in the plan.** Good state secondaries near Cecil Road are oversubscribed and need proof of residential address - while he'll be in temp accommodation until ~Nov and his house is tenanted. **The single most explosive execution gap: he could arrive in Sept with no school place.**
3. **The elderly mother** - demoted from half of Fable's 4-Jul prescription ("near Mum") to one word ("Distant"). Her remaining years are one of only two non-refundable items. London-direct needs an explicit visit cadence, or he's spending that currency unacknowledged.
4. **Search-duration realism** - plan for 9-18 months (age + cohort + gap), not 3-9. Runway absorbs it; the *narrative* must too, or month-10 is where the stall reactivates.

Defence: honest burn numbers now, day-90 tripwire, and a pre-written month-9 review that adjusts *tactics* (rate, tier, Birmingham radius, contracting) not *location*.
