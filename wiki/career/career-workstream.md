## Vision

> *Earn enough to meet my financial goals — that's the primary focus until Sophia finishes university. After that, optimise for autonomy, geographical independence, interesting and rewarding work, and freedom from corporate politics.*

---

## Principles

Hard rules that apply to every Career decision, regardless of what an opportunity offers. Must always adhere.

1. **Protect Health and Work-Life Balance** — Never take a role that creates health-risking stress or carries an expectation of more than 50 hours per week. No compensation package justifies this.
2. **Build Internal and External Alliances** — Actively invest in relationships with key stakeholders, colleagues, and industry contacts. This has been neglected in the past at real cost. Alliances are career infrastructure — build them deliberately, not as an afterthought.
3. **Seek the Intersection of Passion and Income** — When evaluating solopreneur opportunities or career paths, actively prioritize those aligned to genuine interests. Monetizing what I love is the goal, not a bonus. AI is the prime current example: invest in it deeply and find the revenue path from it. The best outcome is work that doesn't feel like work.

---

## Values

What I care about in a career — the "do I want this?" check. Directional guides that shape what good looks like.

1. **Meaning and Purpose** — Work has direction and I genuinely care about it. Purposeful, passionate, and pointed at something that matters.
2. **Recognised and Respected** — I am seen, appreciated, and treated well. Belonging, being valued, and mutual respect are non-negotiable qualities of any working environment.
3. **Empowered and Creative** — Autonomy to make decisions and space to think creatively. Ideally a leadership role that plays to strengths and reduces individual contributor graft. Work aligned to other revenue streams where possible — experience compounds across everything.
4. **Enjoyment and Pride** — Work is genuinely enjoyable and something I am proud of. Western culture, reasonable commute, a platform that sets up future success. If I cannot take pride in what I am doing or who I am doing it for, something is wrong.
5. **Security** — Financial and professional stability as a foundation. A good career funds the life I want and does not put it at risk.
6. **Hard Graft Now, Earned Freedom Later** — Give everything to building financial freedom and career capital until 60. After 60, ramp down to work that is enjoyable, low-stress, and sustainable rather than maximally earning. The sacrifice now buys the freedom later.

## 10-year Ambitions

> *Where I want to be by 2036. Directional and aspirational — not strictly measured, but each should have a loose sense of "how would I know I'm on track?"*

- **Fully Retired from Traditional Employment** — No more corporate roles, no bosses, no politics, no fixed hours. Employment as a necessity is over.
- **Passion-Driven Income** — Either actively generating revenue in a low-stress, geographically independent, intellectually stimulating way aligned to genuine interests — or fully set up and ready to start. Something like tech blogging or commentary. Ideally this has begun before 2036; at minimum the infrastructure, audience, and model are in place so it can launch immediately.

---

## 5-year Directions

> *Where I need to be by 2031 to be on track for the 10-year Ambitions. More grounded — aligned to the Ambitions above.*

- **Maximise Earnings Toward Financial Freedom** — Every career decision over the next 5 years is evaluated primarily through the lens of income. Earn as much as possible to reach financial freedom by 2031. This is the dominant priority until that threshold is hit.
- **Secure Earnings That Cover Basic Costs** — At minimum, ensure income covers living costs so that savings are not eroded. This is the floor - adhering to the Finance "Protect the Floor" principle and maintaining the position achieved. Not eating into savings is non-negotiable regardless of which income path is active.
- **Build Solopreneur Revenue Streams** — Actively incubate or pursue solopreneur activities alongside employment, targeting aggregate ARR of HK$200K. Viable structures include solopreneur, or partnership roles as PM, CTO, or Sales where products and/or ARR allow scaling. Must be geographically independent. These serve three roles: supplementary income when salary is lower than target; primary income if employment doesn't materialise; and the seed of the Passion-Driven Income ambition by 2036.
