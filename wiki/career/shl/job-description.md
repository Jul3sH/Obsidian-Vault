# Job Description - Solution Architect, SHL

## About the job

Job Description

Find purpose in each day while contributing to a workplace revolution! SHL, People Science. People Answers.

Join SHL in redefining the $10B Talent Intelligence market, unlocking new opportunities for growth and innovation. As organizations worldwide look to identify the skills of the future and the impact of AI, SHL can offer its clients end-to-end, data-driven workforce insights that power smarter people outcomes.

As our new Solution Architect, you'll have the chance to guide strategic partnerships, uncover new opportunities for expansion and shape the future of SHL's success. You'll turn satisfied clients into long-term strategic partners and make a measurable impact across our global business.

An excellent benefits package is offered in a culture where career development, collaboration and diversity and inclusivity are all intrinsic to who we are. There's no better time to join SHL as we continue our transformational growth journey. Hybrid working is available.

## What You Will Be Doing

- Lead structured discovery engagements to understand client objectives and diagnose environments across HR processes, technology, data flows, and operating models.
- Translate complex business challenges into clear, scalable solution approaches aligned to SHL's SaaS capabilities.
- Architect end-to-end solutions across SHL's portfolio, covering assessments, platforms, integrations, data flows, and deployment approach.
- Map client requirements to standard product capabilities, maximising reuse and scalability while minimising bespoke delivery.
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Sales, Account Management, Product, Engineering, and Delivery teams to ensure solution feasibility and alignment from presales through execution.
- Design and deliver compelling solution narratives, demos, and proposals, articulating clear value propositions and ROI to diverse stakeholders.
- Partner with commercial teams to shape solution scope, pricing inputs, and RFP responses, supporting conversion from opportunity to contract.
- Ensure seamless handover to deployment teams, maintaining continuity and acting as an escalation point to safeguard solution integrity and client confidence.

## What we are looking for from you

### Essential
- Bachelor's degree in Industrial / Organizational Psychology, Human Factors, Data Science, or a related field (Master's preferred).
- 3-7 years' experience in presales, solution architecture, or solution consulting within SaaS HR Tech, Talent Tech, or Psychometrics.
- Strong understanding of psychometric assessments and their application across the talent lifecycle.
- Proven experience designing solutions across multi-module SaaS platforms and enterprise client environments.

### Desirable
- Familiarity with HR ecosystems (ATS, HCM, LMS, Talent Marketplaces) and integration concepts.
- Comfortable engaging with senior HR, TA, IT, and Procurement stakeholders.
- Excellent communication, storytelling, and facilitation skills.
- Languages - English, Cantonese (preferred) and Japanese (optional).

You must hold the relevant right-to-work document for this Hong Kong based role.
