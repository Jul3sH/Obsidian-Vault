# SHL - Communications Log

> *History of communications regarding the SHL Solution Architect opportunity, surfaced via Cass. Newest entries at the bottom.*

## Background

- Cass is a Managing Director at SHL, a friend of Julian's, brought into a senior position by another friend he has worked for many times.
- SHL's Head of Solution Architects visited Hong Kong and was introduced to Julian by Cass. Julian got on well with both Cass and the Head of Solution Architects.
- Cass introduced Julian on the basis that a Solution Architect role might come up in Hong Kong, and that it would be worth making contact.

## Timeline

### Mid/late May 2026 - In-person catch-up
Met Cass in person. Cass had been drinking and had been speaking with another mutual friend beforehand. Cass was judgemental about Julian having postponed a Cisco interview, despite the postponement having been agreed with Cisco (the coordinator suggested moving the technical interview after the hiring manager round, which had gone well).

### 28 May 2026 - Job posting forwarded
Cass forwarded the SHL Solution Architect job posting to Julian via LinkedIn (see [[job-description]]). Julian noticed Cass had sent and then deleted/retracted an initial message before forwarding it, read as a half-hearted gesture rather than active advocacy.

### 10 June 2026 - Reply drafted (declining)

A reply was drafted in response to Cass's offer to catch up on the weekend, declining to pursue the role rather than waiting. Draft (casual WhatsApp tone, matching the exchange above):

```
Hey Cass, no need to wait for the weekend on this one, I've made a call.

I sat down and ran a proper ATS keyword analysis against the JD (built a tool
for exactly this). Came out at roughly 48% overall match. The gaps aren't just
phrasing either, three of the seven core requirements are the actual domain
itself: psychometrics, SaaS platforms, and HR tech/talent tech. None of that's
in my background. Plus the Cantonese point you mentioned.

On top of that, realistically you and I haven't worked together before, so
there's a natural limit to how far you'd go to bat for me on something this
far outside my lane, and that's completely fair.

Putting those together, I don't think this one's worth pursuing. Appreciate
you thinking of me though, keen to stay in the loop for anything closer to my
actual wheelhouse.
```

See [[decision-log]] for the reasoning behind this decision and [[ats-scoring]] for the ATS analysis referenced in the draft.
