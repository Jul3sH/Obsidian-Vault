# GenAI Solution Architect - Positioning Strategy

> Living strategy doc for the GenAI Solution Architect career target. Parked in the [[funnel|Project Funnel]] (Career) - actively being promoted to a full Project via `/project-planner` (in progress, 17 Jun 2026).
>
> **History:** this doc was initially created as "Forward Deployed Engineer Pivot" on 17 Jun 2026. Market research the same day established that FDE is builder-first (production coding baseline) and that GenAI Solution Architect better monetises Julian's architecture-and-value-first capital. See [[ai-senior-roles]] for the full decision rationale and role comparison.

## Thesis

Target the **GenAI Solution Architect** role (evolving toward **Agentic AI Solution Architect** as proof of work builds).

- GenAI SA is architecture-and-value-first: enterprise use cases, roadmap design, integration, governance, delivery oversight. This is what Julian has done for 20+ years with AI layered on top.
- The coding bar is **fluency** (Python, APIs, cloud patterns, RAG/LLM architecture) not production engineering depth - a gap Julian can close through targeted upskilling and proof of work, not a multi-year retraining.
- FDE and Agentic Engineer were considered and rejected: both are builder-first, requiring a production engineering baseline Julian does not have. See [[ai-senior-roles]].
- Differentiators most GenAI SA candidates lack: deep BFSI vertical credibility (15+ years), US$170M+ in presales-generated revenue, C-level trusted-advisor track record, CISSP/GRC/CTBME depth, and US$300M+ transformation delivery - exactly the "has turned enterprise use cases into secure, integrated, operational solutions" credential the role rewards.

**Positioning narrative:** *"Enterprise solution architect who can also build the agent, not just specify it."* Lead with architecture, governance, and delivery capital; use the agentic build skills (the sabbatical AI OS: tool-using agents, orchestrated workflows, Claude-as-runtime) as the hands-on differentiator.

**Evolution path:** GenAI SA now → Agentic AI SA as published proof of work builds (orchestration frameworks, tool-using agents, evaluation, real client engagements).

**Constraints:** no role that requires >50h/week or is incompatible with HK base. Target remote-friendly or APAC enterprise-AI roles where seniority enables leadership rather than grind.
