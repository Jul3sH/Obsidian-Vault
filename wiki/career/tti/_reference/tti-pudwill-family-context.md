**Relationship:** Best friend from school (1985 onwards); brother of Stephan; son of Horst and Barbara

### Key Context
- Met Julian around 1985; became very good friends while at school
- Part of a tight trio: Chris, Justin (Gilbert), and Julian — spent formative years together

## The 12-Year Absence and Re-Engagement

**1995–2012:** Julian lived overseas; minimal contact with Horst or Barbara beyond Chris's funeral in 2008

**2012 onwards:** Julian returned to Hong Kong

**2012–2014 (early years back):** Horst and Barbara used to take Chris's close friends (including Julian, Justin) out on 11 November each year to commemorate Chris

**~2014 — Social setback with Stephan:** When Julian returned to HK, Stephan's wife Tanya (Russian, similar age to Julian's then-wife Clodagh) was keen to form a social friendship between the couples. Clodagh was not willing to invest in the relationship. Julian believes this created some distance — Stephan had been actively trying to grow the friendship (christening attendance, Thailand visit), and his wife's warm overture went unreciprocated.

**2021 — Cancer and the friendship rebuilt:**
- Julian was diagnosed with cancer in December 2020 and underwent treatment January–May 2021, followed by a recovery period
- Stephan was very active during this period — called regularly to check on Julian
- This is the clearest expression of Stephan's empathetic (Barbara-like) personality, and the period where the Julian–Stephan friendship genuinely deepened again after years of relative distance

**Recent years (2023–2026):** The annual gatherings now centre on Vipa (Chris's widow) at her house; Horst and Barbara attend but less frequently. The Dec 2024 Christmas party (Julian's first invitation) marked the full re-entry into the family's inner circle.

**20 Jun 2026 — Vipa's party / Barbara-radar activation:** At a birthday party for one of Vipa's daughters (Vipa has two grown daughters from Chris plus another daughter from a different partner), Barbara was present. Justin (childhood-trio friend, now an active promoter for Julian) told Barbara that Julian is at a crossroads and may have to leave HK. Barbara - aware Julian had been out of work ~12 months - asked "maybe he could do something at TTI?" Justin replied that this is exactly what Julian has been discussing with Stephan, but no decision has landed. This places the matter on Barbara's radar; she may have seen Stephan on Sun 21 Jun. Textbook family-radar internal-pressure mechanism (see Horst–Stephan dynamic below) - deploy invisibly, never reference in Julian's direct messages to Stephan.

## Current Family Dynamics

### Horst (Chairman, now 82–83) — The Operator
- Self-made man, negotiator, very good at getting people to do things for him — his core operating mode
- Still chairman but hands-off, enjoying his last years
- Was/is hard on Stephan in boardroom settings (calls him out, expresses disappointment)
- When interested in something, makes it happen — his nature
- Recent signals: increasingly warm toward Julian (Christmas parties last 2 years, Sevens box 2026)

### Barbara (Horst's wife) — The Family Heart
- Warm, nostalgic, empathetic, forms genuine bonds
- Julian gets on well with her — this is why recently invited to Christmas parties
- **Rekindling moment (Dec 2024 Christmas party):** Long personal conversation; Julian went down to see Chris's ashes (buried in a wall in the house)
- Met Sophia at Sevens box 2026; nostalgic about Julian teaching Stephan maths
- Acts as the social gatekeeper within the family circle

### Stephan (Vice-Chairman) — More Like Barbara Than Horst
- Younger brother of Chris (age gap meant not as close to Julian growing up)
- Julian taught him maths as a boy; **Fred took over** when Julian stopped, and Fred and Stephan have been close ever since
- **Personality:** Empathetic, kind — more like Barbara than Horst. This is critical: Stephan does not have Horst's operator/negotiator drive
- Risk-averse: combination of natural temperament (Barbara-like) and learned caution (Horst was hard on him in boardroom — actual language was "Stephan is fucking useless," not the cleaned-up version)
- **Role:** Vice-Chairman is administrative — closer to **token / going-through-the-motions** than active operating leadership. Comes into the office a few times a week. Unlikely to become Chairman (public company, investor confidence). Widespread internal frustration in TTI with his inability to get things done.
- **Not motivated by money** — due to inherit substantial wealth whether he takes professional risks or not. The standard career-progression / cost-takeout / win-the-deal levers don't apply to him.
- **His real energy is personal and family.** Major current focus: his son Alexander's squash career — world-class coaches living in one of his HK houses, Alexander training daily with the best in the world, ambition of Hong Kong Olympic representation. This is where his decisive, sustained, hands-on energy actually goes.
- Trusts Julian as a confidant and friend; trusts Fred as an older-brother figure
- **What moves him to act: empathy, not business.** Cancer-period sustained calling (2021), Sophia christening attendance, Sophia hosting at his house, Sevens tracking-of-personal-updates, Feb 2026 Sophia-triggered Ty introduction — the pattern is consistent. Empathy is the lever; business case is the credible artefact he needs to act on once empathy is activated.

### The Horst–Stephan Power Dynamic (observed)
- Even into adulthood, Horst still orders Stephan around in social settings — directing him to fetch water at the Christmas party, etc. (Julian's direct observation)
- Stephan retains a degree of lingering deference and mild fear of his father — visible in body language and compliance with small directives
- **Strategic mechanism:** anything that appears on Horst's radar that Stephan is seen as not addressing creates internal pressure on Stephan to act
- **Why it matters now (May 2026):** Peter has just placed Julian back on Horst's radar (28 May lunch). When Horst returns from Germany and meets Stephan (~7–8 Jun), even a light family-channel mention will activate this dynamic. The lever does not need to be weaponized — Stephan's internal pressure-machine handles the rest.
- **Implication for Peter's parallel conversation:** Peter should lightly signal that Horst is aware, NOT relay any directive from Horst. The mechanism works through Stephan's own response to dad-awareness, not through felt enforcement.

### The Operator/Empath Split — Why It Matters
- Horst and Barbara are *opposites in temperament* — Horst is the driver, Barbara is the heart
- Stephan inherited Barbara's empathetic kindness, not Horst's operator drive
- This means: Stephan opens doors warmly but does not push outcomes the way Horst does
- It is not weakness — it is a different personality wiring inside a family that operates at the highest commercial level
- Practical implication: an ask that requires *decisiveness* needs Horst; an ask that requires *trust and access* runs through Stephan
