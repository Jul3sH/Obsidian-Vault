

**Advertised: 2nd April**

## About the job

### Job Overview:

We are seeking an experienced Principal System Administrator to lead the design, operation and continuous improvement of our core infrastructure platforms across data centers and enterprise environments. 

This role acts as the senior technical authority for infrastructure, providing architectural direction, governance and hands-on expertise in servers, storage, virtualization, networking, security and related enterprise platforms. Cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, etc.) are a plus but not the primary focus. Exposure to AI technologies (such as LLMs and AI agents) is beneficial but not mandatory. 

You will work closely with IT leadership, security, applications and business stakeholders to ensure our infrastructure is secure, reliable, scalable, and aligned with business needs. Candidate with less experience will be considered as Senior System Administrator.

## Responsibilities:

### Infrastructure Architecture & Strategy

- Define and own the overall infrastructure architecture and roadmap covering on‑premises, servers, storage, virtualization, networking, backup / DR and related platforms
- Evaluate and introduce new infrastructure technologies, tools and standards to improve availability, performance, and eﬃciency
- Establish and maintain infrastructure design standards, patterns and best practices(naming, segmentation, security baselines, hardening, monitoring etc.)
- Partner with security and compliance teams to ensure infrastructure meets regulatory, audit, and corporate governance requirements

### Platform Engineering & Operations

Provide expert-level administration and troubleshooting for core platforms such as:
Windows Server and/or Linux (e.g., RHEL) environments
Virtualization platforms (e.g., VMware, Hyper‑V)
Enterprise storage and backup solutions
Directory services and identity platforms (e.g., Active Directory, ADFS, identity federation)
Enterprise monitoring, logging, and configuration management tools
Lead complex incident resolution, root cause analysis and permanent corrective actions
for major infrastructure issues
Oversee capacity planning, performance tuning and life cycle management (patching,
upgrades, decommissioning) of infrastructure components
Define and implement robust backup, restore and disaster recovery strategies, including
regular testing and documentation

### Cloud & Hybrid (Nice to Have but Not Core)

Provide guidance on integrating on‑premises infrastructure with public cloud services

(e.g. Azure, AWS) where applicable

Contribute to hybrid architecture patterns (VPN, ExpressRoute, identity integration, hybrid

DNS etc.)

Support, but not necessarily lead, cloud-related initiatives such as migrating workloads or adopting platform services when relevant to infrastructure

### Automation, IaC, and DevOps Practices

Drive automation of repetitive operational tasks using scripting languages (e.g.

PowerShell, Python, Bash) and / or configuration management tools

Promote Infrastructure as Code (IaC) practices where appropriate (e.g. Ansible,

Terraform) to improve consistency and repeatability

Collaborate with application, DevOps and security teams to streamline environment

provisioning, configuration and deployment workflows

### AI / LLM / AI Agent Exposure (Optional)

Collaborate with data, AI and engineering teams to understand infrastructure needs for AI workloads (e.g. GPU servers, high-performance storage, specialized networking)

Provide infrastructure support for proof-of-concept or pilot deployments of LLMs, AI
agents and related platforms and tools

Contribute to discussions on observability, security and capacity planning for AI workloads, while learning and evolving with the technology

### Leadership, Governance & Collaboration

Act as the principal technical leader and escalation point for the infrastructure team,
including Senior System Administrators

Mentor and coach team members, review designs and changes and ensure technical
quality and adherence to standards

Lead or participate in architecture and change advisory boards, ensuring changes are
assessed for risk, performance and resilience

Communicate effectively with IT management and business stakeholders on
infrastructure strategy, risks and major initiatives

## Requirements:

### Education & Experience

Bachelor degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field; equivalent

experience will also be considered

Typically 10+ years of experience in system administration / infrastructure engineering,

including several years in a senior or lead role

Proven track record designing, implementing and operating mid‑to‑large scale enterprise

infrastructure environments

### Technical Skills – Core

Strong expertise in:

Windows Server and/or Linux administration

Virtualization platforms (e.g., VMware vSphere, Hyper‑V)

Storage technologies (SAN/NAS), backup and recovery solutions

Active Directory, Group Policy, DNS, DHCP , PKI

Networking fundamentals (routing, switching, firewalls, VPNs, load balancing) from an

infrastructure perspective

Experience with high availability and clustering solutions

Strong understanding of security best practices for infrastructure components

(hardening, patching, vulnerability remediation)

Solid experience with scripting and automation (e.g. PowerShell, Bash, Python)

### Technical Skills – Nice to Have

Experience with public cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, or others), especially in hybrid

infrastructure scenarios

Experience with Infrastructure as Code (e.g. Ansible, Terraform) or configuration

management tooling

Exposure to AI / ML infrastructure considerations such as GPU computing,

high-performance storage and orchestration of AI workloads

Familiarity with modern observability stacks (metrics, logs, traces) and SIEM platforms

### Soft Skills

Strong analytical, troubleshooting and problem-solving abilities

Ability to translate business requirements into infrastructure solutions and communicate

trade-offs clearly

Excellent communication, documentation and stakeholder management skills

Demonstrated ability to lead by influence, drive consensus and work effectively in

cross-functional teams

## Certifications (Preferred, Not Mandatory)
