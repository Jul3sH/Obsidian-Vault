# Correction — Don't assume Stephan's reading medium

## What the correction was
While reasoning about how to format the TTI AI Enablement Strategy artefact,
I asserted that Stephan Pudwill "reads on his phone." Julian corrected this:
there is **not enough evidence** to claim this about Stephan. Do not write it
into his stakeholder profile or use it as a planning assumption.

## Why it matters
Stephan is the primary first reader / forwarder of the TTI artefacts. Building
format and length decisions on an unsupported behavioural assumption (phone
reading → must be short) could steer the artefact in the wrong direction.

## How to apply
- Do NOT state or imply Stephan reads on his phone.
- It IS supported that he should be able to grasp the core argument in a couple
  of minutes and feel confident forwarding it onward — design for that, not for
  an assumed device/medium.
- General lesson: do not invent stakeholder traits. Only assert what the wiki
  evidence supports.
