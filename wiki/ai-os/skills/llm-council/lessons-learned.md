## L1 — A probe that *names* an uncertainty is not one that *resolves* it (TTI, Jun 2026)

**What happened:** A council on a stalled job/consulting engagement correctly named the core uncertainty (*"is the intermediary a channel that actually converts?"*) but then recommended an action (*"extract a name from him"*) that **depended on** that uncertainty being resolved favourably — and no gate caught the contradiction. The recommendation reproduced a past failure (an earlier referral by the same intermediary that died from a non-committal hand-off). The flaw was only found later, by a human who knew how the intermediary actually behaves.

**Root causes:**
1. **Lossy brief.** The framing was a ~30-second synthesis of a deep, multi-file history. The two load-bearing facts — the nearest past failure, and a behavioural prior about how the intermediary acts on referrals — never reached the advisors. The council reasoned about the *compression*, not the corpus.
2. **Frame capture.** The synthesis embedded the briefer's own emerging hypothesis, presented as neutral, so advisors metabolised it instead of attacking it.
3. **No root-cause gate.** Peer review and the chairman graded argument quality and surface blind spots, never *"does this recommendation resolve the stated root cause, and would it survive the nearest precedent?"*

**Disciplines now baked into the skill:**
- Tier the briefing depth to the stakes; on high-stakes runs read the full corpus, not 2-3 files (Step 1 tier gate).
- Always surface the **nearest past failure** and **behavioural priors** in the brief (Step 1C/1D items 5).
- State your **own hypothesis separately, flagged for attack** (Step 1D item 6).
- The chairman must pass a **Root-Cause & Precedent Check** before recommending (Step 4); peer reviewers now ask root-cause-vs-symptom (Step 3 Q4).

**The general rule:** more context *volume* doesn't save you — the *right* facts do, the disconfirming precedent above all. **Curation beats volume.** And a recommendation that doesn't visibly resolve the uncertainty you named, or that can't explain why it won't repeat the nearest past failure, is not finished.
