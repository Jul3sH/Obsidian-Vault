# Project: Conduct 3 SBI Feedback Conversations

## What this delivers
**Key Result:** Deliver structured feedback (SBI model) to all 3 direct reports by June 30

## Definition of Done
- Schedule 1-on-1 feedback conversation with each of 3 direct reports (1 hour each)
- Deliver SBI-model feedback in each conversation (specific situation, observed behavior, impact)
- Document the feedback given in brief notes
- Allow for response/questions
- Confirm understanding and next steps

All 3 conversations completed by June 30.

## WSJF Scoring
| Component | Score | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Value | 3 | Gives direct reports clear, actionable feedback. Builds trust. Directly improves their performance and engagement. High career and team impact. |
| Time Criticality | 3 | Mid-year review window. Delayed feedback becomes stale and loses impact. One-month delay means feedback happens after mid-year reviews, losing relevance. |
| Risk / Opportunity | 2 | Enables better engagement in subsequent 1-on-1s (KR2). Direct reports who feel heard are more receptive to development conversations. |
| Job Size | S = 2 | 3–8h (estimated 3–4h): Three 1-hour conversations + 30 min prep per conversation. |
| **WSJF** | **4.0** | (3 + 3 + 2) ÷ 2 = 4.0 |

## Effort Estimate
**Focused time:** 3–4 hours (over 2 weeks, late May to June 15)  
**Calendar time:** 4 weeks (scheduling, conducting, reflecting)

## Execution Plan
1. Schedule 3 feedback conversations by May 20 (book 1-hour slots)
2. Prep notes for each conversation (who, what behavior, what impact) by May 28
3. Conduct conversations May 29 – June 15 (spread them out; don't do all 3 in one week)
4. Document outcome and notes for each
5. Debrief with manager or mentor if helpful

## Notes
This project directly delivers KR1. Success here sets the tone for stronger leadership presence and team trust. Don't rush; each conversation deserves preparation and space for the direct report to respond.
