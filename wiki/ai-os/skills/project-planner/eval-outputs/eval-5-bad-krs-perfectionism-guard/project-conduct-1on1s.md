# Project: Schedule & Conduct 6 Structured 1-on-1s

## What this delivers
**Key Result:** Complete 6 structured 1-on-1s with documented action items and follow-up by July 31

## Definition of Done
- Schedule 2 structured 1-on-1s with each direct report (1 hour each = 6 total)
- Use the 1-on-1 template designed in Project 1
- Document the following in each conversation:
  - Individual update / celebration
  - Progress on prior actions (if any)
  - One deep-dive topic
  - Agreed next actions
- Capture notes and action items
- Close each 1-on-1 with clear follow-up (what happens next)

All 6 conversations completed by July 31.

## Effort Estimate
**Focused time:** 7–8 hours (over 6 weeks, June–July)  
**Calendar time:** 8 weeks (scheduling, conducting, follow-up)

## Execution Plan
1. Finalize 1-on-1 template (Project 1) and share with team by early June
2. Schedule all 6 conversations by June 15 (2 per direct report)
3. Conduct conversations June 20 – July 25 (1 every week or two)
4. Document notes and action items within 24 hours of each
5. Follow up on any actions by the following 1-on-1
6. Debrief with manager or mentor if needed

## Template Structure
- **Opening (3 min):** How are you doing? Any wins to celebrate?
- **Updates (5 min):** What's on your mind? What's working well?
- **Prior Actions (5 min):** How did the things we agreed on last time go?
- **Deep Dive (10 min):** Let's focus on [topic]. What's your perspective?
- **Next Actions (5 min):** What will we do before we meet again?
- **Closing (2 min):** Thanks. Looking forward to next time.

## Notes
This project directly delivers KR2. Regular, structured 1-on-1s are the cornerstone of effective leadership. Consistency matters more than perfection — if a 1-on-1 is cut short, reschedule rather than skip.
