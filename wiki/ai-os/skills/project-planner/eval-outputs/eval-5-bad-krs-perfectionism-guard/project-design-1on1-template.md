# Project: Design 1-on-1 Template & Agenda

## What this delivers
**Key Result:** Complete 6 structured 1-on-1s with documented action items and follow-up by July 31

## Definition of Done
A documented 1-on-1 template (1–2 page guide) that includes:
- Opening ritual (3 min)
- Individual updates / celebration (5 min)
- Progress on prior actions (5 min)
- Deep dive on 1–2 topics (10 min)
- Agreed next actions (5 min)
- Closing (2 min)

Template shared with direct reports before first 1-on-1.
