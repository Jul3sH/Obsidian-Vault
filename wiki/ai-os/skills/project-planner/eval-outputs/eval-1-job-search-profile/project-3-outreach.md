# Build List and Conduct Peer Outreach

## What this delivers

**Key Result:** Conduct 5–10 informational interviews with peers by July 31

## Definition of Done

- List of 5–10 senior peers identified (from network, LinkedIn, past colleagues)
- Personalized outreach emails/messages sent to each person with a clear ask (informational interview)
- At least 5 positive responses received and interviews scheduled
- Outreach complete by end of May to allow June interviews
