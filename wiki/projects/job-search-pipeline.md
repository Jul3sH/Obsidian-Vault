## Objective

Maintain a continuous pipeline of qualified senior role applications in Hong Kong — trawling job sites, screening inbound, tailoring resumes, and submitting cold applications — so no opportunity is missed while active opportunities progress as separate projects.

## Scope

**Standing project.** Covers everything up to and including submitting a cold application or accepting a warm intro for a first screen. Once an opportunity progresses beyond that point (interview advance, relationship-led engagement requiring bespoke prep), it spins up as its own bounded project.

**What belongs here:**
- Job board trawling and opportunity screening
- Initial ATS check and go/no-go decision
- Tailored resume production for cold/standard applications
- Submitting the application

**What belongs in a separate project:**
- Any opportunity that advances to interview or requires significant relationship management, bespoke strategy, or multi-deliverable preparation

## Outcomes / Outputs

1. Output: Each qualified cold opportunity receives a tailored, evidence-backed application within the same timebox it is found
2. Output: At least one opportunity per month screened and actioned (applied or consciously passed)
