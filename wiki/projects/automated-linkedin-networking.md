## Objective
Have a live LinkedIn networking system that builds and warms a pipeline of relevant industry contacts now, so warm relationships are already in place to convert into advocacy and introductions once the brand/KPI foundation lands.

## Outcomes / Outputs
1. Output: Automation live on a contact's existing blueprint, running on a recurring cadence
2. Outcome: ≥30 warm contacts at target companies by end of Q3 2026

## Hypothesis
> *We believe running an automated LinkedIn networking system on a contact's existing blueprint will build a warm pipeline of ≥30 relevant industry contacts at target companies by end of Q3 2026. We'll know this is working when we see external advocacy signals - profile views, replies, intros - from that pipeline.*

## Confidence
High - the headline outcome (≥30 warm contacts) is largely within our control given the existing blueprint; external advocacy is a watched experiment on top, not the gate.

## Future Leverage
Reusable for [[cold-outreach-real-estate|Cold Outreach — Real Estate]] and other business-development outreach - an independent option, not a fallback.

## Existing Leverage
A contact's existing LinkedIn networking blueprint de-risks the design (doesn't shrink build effort).

## Leading Indicators
External advocacy signals (profile views, replies, intros) appearing from the warmed pipeline within the first timebox - watched, not gated; Confidence (High) rests on the ≥30-contact outcome regardless of whether advocacy materialises early.
