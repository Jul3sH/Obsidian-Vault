## Objective
Prove that I can generate qualified real estate investor leads at a cost far below market rate, giving my friend the confidence to partner with me and creating a new income stream.

## Outcomes / Outputs
1. Generate 20 qualified leads (understands the offer, has capital to invest, wants a meeting) from a 2,000-contact list within 2 months — ≥1% conversion rate
2. Cost per qualified lead must be substantially cheaper than HK$1,000 (current market benchmark)
3. By end of month 2, the full end-to-end pipeline is invokable using LLM skills (minimal manual steps)

## Hypothesis

> *We believe personalised AI-generated cold emails to Hong Kong property developers will generate qualified discovery call bookings at a conversion rate of ≥1% from a curated 2,000-contact list. We'll know this is true when the first campaign batch achieves measurable reply and booking rates within week 1 of sending.*
