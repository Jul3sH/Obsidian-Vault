# Send Cold Email Sequence to 50 Real Estate Prospects

## Story
> *As a sales consultant, I want to send a cold email sequence to 50 new real estate prospects this week so that I can book 3 discovery calls before the end of the month (my quota resets and I'll miss my commission target if I don't hit 3 calls).*

## Acceptance Criteria
1. 50 prospects identified from the lead list
2. Personalised emails sent to all 50 prospects
3. Follow-up sequence active for all non-responders

## Definition of Done
50 prospects are in an active email sequence. At minimum 3 replies received or discovery calls booked by month end.
