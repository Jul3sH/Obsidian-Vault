# Email Campaign

## Story
> *As a business developer, I want to configure and run a personalised cold email campaign via Saleshandy aligned to the agreed strategy, and review initial results, so that I can generate qualified leads for Adam's real estate investment proposition.*

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Saleshandy campaign is configured with the correct contact list, email sequence, and sending schedule aligned to the agreed strategy
2. Campaign is live and emails are being delivered (no bounce/deliverability blockers)
3. Open rates, reply rates, and bounce rates are being tracked in Saleshandy
4. At least one A/B test has been run (subject line, email body, or call-to-action variant) with results recorded
5. At least one review cycle completed — results assessed against strategy targets and at least one adjustment made based on data

## Definition of Done
The cold email campaign is live in Saleshandy, configured per the agreed strategy with the correct contact list and personalised sequences. At least one A/B test has been run and results reviewed. A data-driven adjustment has been made, and open rates, reply rates, and bounce rates are being actively tracked. Adam has been briefed on initial results.

## Notes
- Blocked by: Exploration Enabler (B2C risk assessment → go/no-go → strategy definition)
- Campaign type (B2B vs B2C vs hybrid) determined by enabler outcome
- Runs alongside or replaces B2C path depending on risk assessment result
