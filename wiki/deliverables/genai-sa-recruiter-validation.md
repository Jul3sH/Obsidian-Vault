# GenAI SA Recruiter Validation

## Task Description
Capture recruiter input on whether a GenAI SA positioning will materially improve Julian's employability — pressure-testing the provisional verdict from the market research against people who actually hire or place these roles, and producing a confirmed go/no-go decision.

## Completion Criteria
1. ≥3 recruiters or hiring contacts (active in GenAI/enterprise AI architect placement in HK and/or UK) consulted on whether the pivot is viable for someone with Julian's background
2. Their input captured in writing (in or linked from the market research doc): does the positioning materially help, what's the real gap, what would they want to see
3. A confirmed go/no-go decision documented — the provisional verdict upheld or revised in light of recruiter input

## Definition of Done
Input from ≥3 relevant recruiters is captured in writing alongside the market research, and the provisional go/no-go verdict has been confirmed or revised into a final decision. Julian can state, with evidence, whether the GenAI SA pivot is a "go" worth investing Phase 2 effort into.
