# GenAI SA Market Research

## Task Description
Conclude a provisional go/no-go decision on the GenAI SA pivot, backed by structured market research — job listings, market reports, and industry papers across HK and UK, researched via Gemini deep research and Perplexity and synthesised by Claude. The decision is the deliverable; the research is what makes it credible.

## Completion Criteria
1. A documented **provisional go/no-go verdict** exists: is the GenAI SA positioning credible, and is there sufficient demand to justify investing in Phase 2 — or is it a hobby that won't move the employment needle?
2. wiki/career/genai-sa-market-research.md exists and backs the verdict with: demand landscape (HK + UK), required skills by seniority, the gap against Julian's current profile, and the employer/outsourcer landscape
3. ≥10 job listings reviewed in HK and ≥10 in UK (minimum 20 total; go higher if the research warrants it)
4. ≥3 market/industry reports or papers reviewed from firms that hire or place GenAI SA talent

## Definition of Done
wiki/career/genai-sa-market-research.md exists with a structured synthesis of ≥20 job listings and ≥3 industry reports across HK and UK, covering demand, required skills by seniority, the profile gap, and the employer/outsourcer landscape. The document concludes with a clear, defensible **provisional go/no-go verdict** on the GenAI SA pivot — the input that recruiter validation (the next deliverable) then confirms or challenges.
