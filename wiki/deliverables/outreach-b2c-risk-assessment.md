# B2C Risk & Strategy Assessment

## Enabler Type
**Exploration** — a time-boxed research spike to resolve a legal showstopper (B2C cold email without opt-in is illegal) and identify a viable legal path before committing execution stories.

## Technical Objective
Assess B2C legal constraints, evaluate B2B viability and a hybrid B2B-addressed approach as potential workarounds, produce a go/no-go decision for each path, and define the target segment and outreach strategy ready for execution.

## What This Unblocks
- **Lead Creation story** — what kind of leads to build depends entirely on the chosen path
- **Email Campaign story** — campaign type, targeting, and copy strategy all depend on this decision
- **Entire Epic** — nothing executes until the legal/strategic question is resolved

## Success Criteria
1. B2C legal constraints documented — opt-in requirements, jurisdiction risks, and whether the hybrid B2B-addressed approach legally circumvents them
2. Hybrid approach researched — at least one concrete example of copy/targeting strategy documented (e.g. targeting investment-adjacent firms like finance companies) with a legal viability verdict
3. Go/no-go decision recorded for each of the three paths (pure B2B, pure B2C, hybrid), with rationale — Adam and Julian aligned
4. Target segment and outreach strategy defined for the chosen path(s), documented at `wiki/deliverables/outreach-b2c-risk-assessment.md` and ready to hand off to Lead Creation and Email Campaign stories

## Definition of Done
The B2C legal constraint has been assessed and documented, including the viability of a hybrid B2B-addressed approach as a potential workaround. A go/no-go decision exists for each of the three paths (pure B2B, pure B2C, hybrid), with Adam and Julian aligned on the chosen direction. The resulting target segment and outreach strategy are documented and ready to hand off — unblocking Lead Creation and Email Campaign.

## Research Approach
- Primary: Perplexity deep research on email compliance law (GDPR, CAN-SPAM, CASL, HK PDPO)
- Validation: Gemini cross-check on findings
- Hybrid path: research whether B2B-addressed emails to investment-adjacent firms (finance, wealth management, IFAs) legally reach individual investors without triggering B2C opt-in requirements
