# TTI — Consulting Brief

## Story
> *As a consultant, I want a 2-page Google Doc brief positioning me as a versatile, AI-leveraged, cross-functional program delivery resource — able to work across multiple stakeholders and initiatives driven out of America — so that Stephan sees consulting as a practical mechanism to get senior AI delivery capability without a headcount commitment.*

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Brief positions Julian as a versatile, AI-leveraged delivery resource capable of managing cross-functional programs across multiple stakeholders and initiatives — with the efficiency case made explicit
2. Brief frames consulting as a headcount-flexible model for fulfilling senior AI responsibilities — positioned as applicable beyond just this engagement
3. Brief includes a concise summary of Julian's key capability areas, noting these were shared with Stephan previously, to ground the consulting offer in demonstrated expertise
4. Brief includes a clear ask or proposed next step
5. Document is 2 pages or fewer in Google Docs; Julian is satisfied it's ready to present or send to Stephan

## Definition of Done
A 2-page Google Doc brief exists positioning Julian as a versatile, AI-leveraged, cross-functional delivery resource — covering the consulting-as-headcount-flexibility model and summarising the key capabilities previously shared with Stephan — and closing with a clear ask. Julian is satisfied it's polished and ready to present or send to Stephan.
