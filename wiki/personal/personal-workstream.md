# Personal — Goals

For the methodology behind this structure, see [[ai-os/service-design/workstream-framework|Workstream Framework]].

---

## Vision

> *Have an enjoyable life and be true to myself in the limited time I have left.*

---

## Principles

Hard rules that apply to every Personal decision, regardless of circumstance. Must always adhere.

1. **Non-Negotiable Enjoyment Floor** — Obligation never dominates for any sustained period. If the balance tips beyond 60% obligation at any point, it is a signal to act - not to endure. The floor is protected regardless of financial pressure.
2. **Revenue-Adjacent Hobbies (Grind Phase)** — During the 5-year grind, hobbies must be adjacent to or directly monetizable. If something is enjoyable and can earn, it should. AI is the prime example: pursue it deeply, and find the path to revenue from it. Pure leisure hobbies are not off-limits but should not dominate time investment while financial freedom is still being built.

---

## Values

Core beliefs that define what Personal means and how decisions here are made.

1. **True to Myself** — Make decisions that are true to myself as well as Sophia.
2. **Enjoyment** — Find time to do things that I enjoy.
3. **Exciting and Balanced Life** — Comfortable lifestyle that feels like a permanent holiday. Breaks to rejuvenate. Hobbies and interests as important as work. Lifelong excitement is non-negotiable. The present matters as much as the future - earning freedom is not a licence to defer living entirely.
4. **Do It Now, Not Later** — Actively work through the bucket list while I am healthy, capable, and alive. Deferring experiences until "the right time" is a losing strategy - capability declines, health fails, and time runs out. The window to do the things I want is open now; treat it accordingly.

---

## 10-year Ambitions

> *Where I want to be by 2036. Directional and aspirational — not strictly measured, but each should have a loose sense of "how would I know I'm on track?"*

- **The 80/20 Life** — 80% of time and energy spent on things I genuinely love; obligation reduced to 20% by 2036. The obligation ratio drops in phases across the decade: 60% now, falling to 40%, then 30%, then 20% as financial freedom builds. This is the payoff for the hard work of the preceding years.
- **Hobbies for Their Own Sake** — By 2036 hobbies are chosen for genuine personal interest, not revenue alignment. Ideally pursued with a partner - shared experiences and shared curiosity as part of what makes life good.

---

## 5-year Directions

> *Where I need to be by 2031 to be on track for the 10-year Ambitions. More grounded — aligned to the Ambitions above.*

- **Build the Future Without Mortgaging the Present** — Target 60/40 (enjoyment/obligation) by 2031 - the midpoint of the arc to the 80/20 life. Hard work is dominant now but the enjoyment floor is always protected. All-in is only acceptable when the work is genuinely inspiring and building toward a life worth arriving at.
- **Channel Hobbies Toward Revenue** — Hobbies in this phase should be technology-adjacent and ideally monetizable. Seek the overlap between what I love and what earns. AI is the clearest current example - invest deeply in it and find the path to income from it.
