# Finance — Goals

For the methodology behind this structure, see [[ai-os/service-design/workstream-framework|Workstream Framework]].

---

## Vision

> *Achieve financial freedom within 5 years — able to live anywhere when Sophia goes to university — while never dropping below my current position of security and flexibility.*

---

## Principles

Hard rules that apply to every Finance decision, regardless of circumstance. Must always adhere.

1. **Protect the Floor** — Never drop below my current position of financial security and flexibility. This is non-negotiable.
2. **Sophia's Quality of Life** — Never compromise Sophia's quality of life through a financial decision. Her standard of living is protected regardless of my circumstances.

---

## Values

What I care about financially — directional guides that shape decisions.

1. **Peace of Mind** — Financial security means freedom from anxiety and fear. Money should eliminate worry, not create it.
2. **Maintain Standard of Living** — Sustain and build on the lifestyle achieved. Not just defend a minimum — actively protect the quality of life built so far.
3. **Financial Freedom** — The option to choose. Work is a choice, not a necessity. Time and location are mine to control.
