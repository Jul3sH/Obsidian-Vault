# Sophia - Malvern Clubs and Activities

Football and athletics clubs near Hanley Swan (WR8) suitable for a 12-year-old girl. Relevant to the **Move to Malvern** scenario in the [[uk-relocation-decision|UK Relocation Decision]], and a concrete **mitigation for the autonomy / social-isolation risk** (village life, transport-dependent, remote from friends): local clubs are how Sophia builds a Malvern friend group and keeps an active social world. Closest options first.

## Key Takeaways
- **Football, closest:** Welland Junior FC (~2-3 miles, Welland) has a confirmed U12 Girls team - the standout on the doorstep.
- **Athletics, best fit:** Worcester Athletic Club (~10-11 miles) has a School Years 6/7/8 junior group, Tue & Thu 6-7pm. Nearer running clubs are adults-only.
- **First moves:** contact Welland Junior FC (football) and Worcester AC junior section (athletics).
- Caveat: the official FA and England Athletics finders were down during the search, so this is from club websites / league listings - confirm current teams and spaces directly.

## Girls' Football Clubs (U12/U13)

- **Welland Junior FC** - ~2-3 miles (Welland village, just south of Hanley Swan). FA Charter Standard club with a dedicated girls' section including a confirmed **U12 Girls** team. Plays at Spitalfield, Welland. info@wellandjuniorfc.co.uk / 07768 657870. wellandjuniorfc.org.uk/our-teams/
- **Newtown Sports Junior FC** - ~5-6 miles (Malvern). Biggest junior club in the Malvern area, girls up to U16, entered U12 girls in the Mercian Festival Girls League. Girls train Thursday evenings at The Chase. 0777 378 3017. newtownfootball.co.uk
- **Worcester City FC Girls** - ~8-9 miles (Worcester). Strong setup with both **U12 and U13** girls' teams in Saturday leagues, playing at Perdiswell. worcestercityfc.org/youth/
- **Perdiswell Colts FC** - ~9 miles (Worcester). U12 (Kestrels) and U13 (Cougars) girls' teams in the Mercian Fortis league. ctperdifc@outlook.com

Further out: Ashton 88 FC, Evesham Town Juniors (U12 girls), Tewkesbury Town Colts (~13 mi), Kidderminster Harriers Girls (~15 mi, U8-U14).

## Athletics Clubs (junior section, age 12)

- **Worcester Athletic Club** - ~10-11 miles (Worcester). Thriving junior section from age 10+, with a **School Years 6/7/8 group** (fits a 12-year-old). Training **Tue & Thu 6-7pm** at Nunnery Wood Sports Centre. Full track and field, cross country, indoor athletics. Junior membership £65/year. Gill Repton, youngathletes@worcester-ac.co.uk. worcester-ac.co.uk/Membership.htm

Nearer running clubs - Malvern Joggers (~5 mi), Pershore Plum Plodders (~7 mi), Ledbury and District Harriers (~8 mi) - are adults-only (18+) with no junior sections, so Worcester AC is the one to go for.

## Suggested next steps
1. **Football:** contact Welland Junior FC first (closest, has a U12 girls team). If timing/spaces don't work, Newtown (Malvern) is next nearest.
2. **Athletics:** email Worcester AC's junior contact about the Year 6/7/8 Tue/Thu sessions.
