# Wellbeing — Goals

For the methodology behind this structure, see [[ai-os/service-design/workstream-framework|Workstream Framework]].

---

## Vision

> *To live a healthy, active and exciting lifestyle of my choice, that is aligned with my core values, and to manage my mind so I feel content, happy, and fulfilled.*

---

## Principles

Hard rules that apply to every Wellbeing decision, regardless of circumstance. Must always adhere.

1. **Never Be a Victim** — Refuse the victim mindset. Life challenges are problems to enjoy solving, not things that happen to me. This stance is non-negotiable.
2. **Moderate Healthy Behaviour Through Guiding Structure** — Use systems and structure to moderate health-impacting behaviour — not willpower alone. Build the guardrails; don't rely on discipline in the moment.

---

## Values

What I care about in wellbeing — directional guides that shape decisions.

1. **Fulfilled and Confident** — Feel great about myself; pursue interests and goals from enjoyment, not desperation. High self-esteem from looking good, feeling good, doing meaningful things well, and exercising signature strengths — leaning into what I am naturally good at.
2. **Healthy and Active** — Mind, body, and soul. Sharpen the saw: exercise consistently, build healthy habits, gym and sports I enjoy, meditation, moderate drinking. Living longer for Sophia and staying successful.
3. **Calm and Resilient** — Manage stress proactively and avoid unnecessary stress. Reframe challenges and focus on positives — what can be controlled, what can be learned, what can move forward.

---

## 10-year Ambitions

> *Where I want to be by 2036. Directional and aspirational — not strictly measured, but each should have a loose sense of "how would I know I'm on track?"*

- **Look 15 Years Younger** — Appearance, skin, physique, and energy signal someone who is 15 years younger than my chronological age.
- **Biological Age 15 Years Younger** — Measurable health markers (VO2 max, resting heart rate, metabolic age, etc.) consistently read 15 years below chronological age.
- **Content, Happy, and Fulfilled** — A settled internal state: not chasing, not anxious, genuinely satisfied with life as it is while still growing.

---

## 5-year Directions

> *Where I need to be by 2031 to be on track for the 10-year Ambitions. More grounded — aligned to the Ambitions above.*

- **Exceptionally Athletic Physique and Health** — Look and perform like an exceptionally athletic 60-year-old: muscular, lean, with a physique equal to or better than today. Measurable health markers (VO2 max, resting heart rate, metabolic age, etc.) tracking toward a biological age 15 years below chronological. Appearance and internal health performance advance together.
- **Cosmetic Interventions Complete** — All planned cosmetic work done and settled: Blepharoplasty, skin tightening and rejuvenation, and where appropriate a facelift and second hair transplant. Result: look younger than 50, natural, not distorted or plastic.
- **Content and Fulfilled** — Settled, comfortable, and genuinely fulfilled. Not chasing, not anxious — a stable internal state that holds across good periods and hard ones.
