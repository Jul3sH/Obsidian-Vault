# SW19 Secondary Schools - London Scenario

State secondary school landscape for Sophia under the **Move to London** scenario in the [[uk-relocation-decision|UK Relocation Decision]]. Researched July 2026 to fill the "Assumptions vs supporting evidence" cells on the SW19 school-admission risk rows in the risk register.

## Key Takeaways
- **Raynes Park is a safe fallback:** not oversubscribed in 2024-25, so any Merton Borough address makes a place near-certain. It is a step down academically but has strong sport (FA Charter Standard, Fulham FC partnership).
- **Ricards Lodge is the prize:** Outstanding girls' school, very oversubscribed (3 apps / place, max ~1.61 miles). Address selection is the lever.
- **Harris Academy is the co-ed Outstanding option:** also oversubscribed, distance-based from 3 nodal points. Apply alongside Ricards Lodge.
- **Ursuline is effectively closed for non-Catholics:** faith criteria fill most Year 7 places. Treat as unavailable unless Sophia is Catholic.
- **The 4 SW19 / Merton schools for Sophia:** Ricards Lodge + Harris Academy + Ursuline + Raynes Park. If she has a Merton address, Raynes Park is the floor.
- **Address selection is the key control:** choose a rental within 1.61 miles of Ricards Lodge (SW19 7HB) and close to a Harris Academy nodal point.

## The Four Schools

### 1. Ricards Lodge High School (SW19 7HB)
- **Girls-only state school, Ofsted Outstanding** (Nov 2023).
- Very oversubscribed: 828 applications for 260 places (3:1). Max distance offered ~1.61 miles.
- Sport: football (field + astroturf), netball, rugby, tennis; ball-girl programme for Wimbledon.
- Admission: non-selective, distance-based after looked-after children. No faith criteria.
- **Address selection is the only lever. No exam. No Catholic requirement.**

### 2. Harris Academy Wimbledon
- **Co-ed academy, Ofsted Outstanding** (2023). Opened 2018, 853 pupils.
- GCSE: top 25% of English schools nationally (Att8 above national midpoint).
- Sport: football, rugby, hockey, netball, cricket, tennis and more - confirmed for girls.
- Oversubscribed: distance-based from three nodal points (Colliers Wood station, school entrance, Haydon's Road station). Apply early and choose address near a nodal point.

### 3. Ursuline High School Wimbledon
- **Girls-only Catholic VA school, Ofsted Good.** 1,390 pupils.
- Faith criteria: Catholics fill almost all Year 7 places. Non-Catholics rarely admitted at Year 7. ~1/5 of total roll is non-Catholic, but that's across all year groups via in-year moves.
- **Treat as unavailable unless Sophia is Catholic.** If applying, provide a strong evidence of faith support for the school's ethos.

### 4. Raynes Park High School (SW20 0JL)
- **Co-ed state school, Ofsted Good** (Nov 2024). 1,207 pupils.
- GCSE: Attainment 8 = 43.6; 43.5% grade 5+ in Maths and English. Below the national average (~50%) and well below Ricards Lodge / Harris.
- Sport (strong): FA Charter Standard Development School Award for outstanding contribution to football. Fulham FC Foundation partnership (girls' football development programme). AFC Wimbledon partnership.
- Admission: **NOT oversubscribed in 2024-25** (200 places, did not fill). Any Merton Borough address gives a near-certain place.
- The academic step-down is real but sport is well covered.

## Beyond SW19 - Contingency Schools

If Sophia misses all 4 Merton schools (very low probability with a Merton address), the nearest strong alternatives are in Wandsworth:

### Graveney School (SW17 9BU, Tooting)
- **Co-ed, Ofsted Outstanding** (Nov 2022). Wandsworth's top performing secondary at A-Level (The Times, 2020).
- GCSE: 74% grade 5+ - significantly stronger than Raynes Park.
- Sport: long track record of local, national and international sporting success.
- **Admission barrier:** 6+ applications per place. Partially selective - 25% of places via the Wandsworth Test (academic ability). The remaining 75% by distance. **Requires a Wandsworth address and/or Wandsworth Test entry.** Cannot be used as a Merton Borough fallback.
