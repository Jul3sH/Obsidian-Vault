Situation: Didn't take advantage of several offers through my network to get jobs, and now out of contract, losing money. 

Root Cause: Insecure, and concerned about losing face by doing badly in the inteviews. I internalised this by mascarading it as several other reasons not to take up the offers, but this was the root cause. 

Lesson Learnt: Must keep pushing forward with two important disciplines. Beating fear, especially the fear of failure, and beating procrastination.
