 Sit: Loads and loads where for one reason or another I have not written up notes immediately after a meeting, and by the time I have got round to it, I can’t remember the detail OR read my writing.

RC: I forget, my writing is crap, and if I am concentrating/contributing to the meeting I will not note everything down and must rely on my memory. Even when I have improved my handwriting, I still need to focus on the meeting which means writing less notes and relying on my memory.

LL: If I am going to write notes, write them that day. This must become a categorical priority. It’s a false economy leaving them because it is more stressful and time consuming in the long run. Remind myself of this when procrastinating over writing the notes.

 Never procrastinate writing up meeting notes, it is categorically a priority
