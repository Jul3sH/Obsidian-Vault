Situation: In two interviews I have been so confident that I didn't really get challenged. 

Root Cause: Confidence, and steering my response to extra detail that I can elaborate on even more confidently, that shows additional depth above and beyond what they asked. I think the engineers are then scared to challenge me in front of their boss in case they get it wrong. 

Lesson Learned: Don't be scared to move the response towards a strength, as long as I don't deviate too obviously, or go on for too long. It's not avoiding the answer, it's displaying additional depth of knowledge.
