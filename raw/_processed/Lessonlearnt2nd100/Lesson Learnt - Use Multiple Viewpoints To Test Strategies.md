Situation: Stephen Green has gone cold on giving me a refernce

root cause: Something has put him off, because he sounded genuinely happy to. It could easily be because I mentioned in my email to Stephen that Gio was going to.

Lesson learnt: I mentioned Gio to Stephen to give him confidence, however, I didn't really think it through. Is he going to just because Gio did, especially when Gio is junior ?  What could easily of happened is that he spoke to Gio, told him his concerns about writing one, and then they both justified the safe not committal approach together. 

I should have thought through the approach from a couple of viewpoints first.
