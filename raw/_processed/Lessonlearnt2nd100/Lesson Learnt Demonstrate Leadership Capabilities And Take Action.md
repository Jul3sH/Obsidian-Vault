**Situation**: numerous telling my boss what I am going to do, rather than what I have done, or what I am doing. 
**Root cause**: I must make it clear what I have achieved, but it is much more impressive to reel off a list of things I have done rather than make a noise about things I am going to do, and if I run the obvious past my boss looking for confirmation it's the right thing to do, he will start to wonder how experienced I am.
**Lesson learnt**:  I am in senior positions now, so I have authority to make these kinds of decisions and must exercise this power. I also need to communicate in cool, calm, collected and assertive statements rather than questions if I ma to discuss what I wil be doing.
