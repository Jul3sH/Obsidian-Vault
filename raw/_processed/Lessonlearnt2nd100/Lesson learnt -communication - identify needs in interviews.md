Situation: didn't get hired for immediate cloud talent work.
Root cause: not skillet required, as I said that my strengths are DC, as that is what I was feeling most confident about at the time. I should have said most things networking, and then asked " if you have a particular piece of work in mind, maybe I can tell you my strengths relative to that piece of work. " I then could have targeted my response to their requirements: 
Lessons learnt: 

1- Establish requirements by listening, reading the situation more rather than just talking. think to myself, what is my objective here, I.e. to find out what they need, and advise I can provide the solution. So I should first of all have confirmed what the objectives were of the discussion. I should then have asked what specific project, and then have answered saying they were my strengths. 

2- Don't under sell myself. Be confident. I can do WAN, and was talking about DC because I was most confident talking about it.
