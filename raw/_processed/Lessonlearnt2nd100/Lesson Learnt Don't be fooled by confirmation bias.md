**Situation**
I was convinced that I was right about the patio needing to be large enough to account for an extension, and was working out ways to convinve cloadgh, when in fact Mum pointed out that they can easily pick up the slabs and move them. 
**Root cause**
Whist I was taking a step back and working out ways to convince Clodagh rather than ramming my opinion down her throat, what I didn't do was consider that I may be wrong, and think about both options with the dame level of creativity. 

**Lesson Leant**
This is a classic case of confirmation Bias**.** Always be careful of this.
