****Situation:**** Gio asked me to do some work. I made a fuss any he got really pissed off. 

**Root Cause:** I was getting concerned that I would become accountable and didn't have time. 

**Lesson Learnt** I let it blow over then apologised "for the way I handled the situation", and then explained my concerns about hearing that he'd  said I don't have any regional commitments getting in the way of DA deliverables. He clearly enjoyed the respect from apologising and I got him back on side. I also did the right thing not doing any of this in an even remotely accusatory way, even though he should not have said what he did. He now knows it will get back to me, but i didn't cause more friction. In fact I offered a solution to help provide more transparency, insinuting that it's not his fault.
