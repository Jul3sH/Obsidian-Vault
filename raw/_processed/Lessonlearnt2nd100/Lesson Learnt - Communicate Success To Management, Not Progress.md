**Situation** Keep telling SM what I have done, in a kind of "look what i've done" way, and him not having much interest. 

**Root Cause** Ken to impress, but not doing it the right way. 

**Lessons learned:** It feels obvious, so it will be obvious, only tell him things if there is a point, and make it obvious that there is a point. short, sweet, succinct, factual, pragmatic statements. Always be telling what I have already done to sort out a problem moving forward ( so not what I just done so sort out where we are at, what I have already done moving forward to the next step.
