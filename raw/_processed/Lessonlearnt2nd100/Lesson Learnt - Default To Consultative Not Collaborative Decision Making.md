****Situation:****
Running everything by the other DAs, and sensing that they are getting frustrated as they have a lot of their own work to do. 

**Root Cause:**
As it's political, I don't want to be speaking in behalf of the team out of line. 

**Lesson Learnt:**
Don't ask question, provide solutions. So say. I suggest the following approach... then bullet point, then ask for agreement. Then it's hopefull just a one word response, and i look lke a more authoritative problem solving leader. Besides, they aren't running everything by me. 

Be decisive, and take a more consultative that collaborative approach by default. This way I am soliciting opinions to be respectful, but demonstrating my ability to meek decisions on my own.
