****Situation:**** Talking to Mark about a project, he appeared to have done nothing, and then huffed and puffed when I started to talk about what needs to happen, followed by complaining that it was all a pain.

**Root Cause:** It looked to me like He'd dropped the ball, and was clearly a bit stressed out because he didn't really know what to do next. 

**Lesson learned:** He may well just have been bored and pissed off by the task, but it made him look weak and unorganised.  I've done this before so don't.  Never react by huffing and puffing, it looks unprofessional and than you can't handle the task.
