situation; When being interviewed by HP I was asked about HP but didn't know much about them. I had leant about their products, but clearly neglected learning about the company. 

root cause: Subjective prioritisation. I had been busy learning technical stuff, which I would have about a 20% hit rate of getting right, because I was so scared of failing technically and losing face. I had researched their products, but not the company itself, and there has been a lot in the news about them too. The reality is that I look stupid having not researched the company i am to work for, especially for a perm role, and even more stupid if I am not curret on news in the industry, especially for a sales role. 

Lesson learnt: Ask myself always, is this the best use of my time, and am I objectively prioritiing as opposed to subjectively, which I have a tendancy to do.
