**Situation**: not getting the required resources to do what we had been tasked to do. 
**Root cause**: With regards to the DA role, it was a project, and we therefor should have treated it as such by highlighting in the issues log each time we had an unsolvable problem, thus making it the RNMs problem. 

What we could have done is raise the issues in the issue log, and then indicate these numbers to Stuart in the Monthly meeting, rather than have to highlight the issue which would have been embarrassing with the RNMs on the call. We should, however, have stood up for ourselves and highlighted the issues. This was the agreed approach. We should have sais to the RNMs "This was athe agreed approach, you haven't provided us with the resource, do we need to take this to Stuart to help us resolve the issue ? " this would then have been above board, would have given them the opportunity to resolve the issue, and also made it clear that they faced an unpleasant alternative if they don't sort it out. 

Additional Constraints need to be highlighted in the issues log. 

**Lesson learnt**: Agree a reporting and escalation path at the beginning of projects or in new positions so I am following the agreed protocol when raising issues, and can 't be accused of making anyone look bad.
