**Situation**: SoI did it again, I had a moan about someone in the office. I knew it would be well received, which is why I did it, because she had a rant about the same person before. I must practice NEVER whinging. 

**Root cause**: I was trying to build rapport. 
Negatives are: she may now quote me to back up her position and this will get out, could get back to that person, who will then defend by pointing out who else I have slated. Others will think that's Julian whinging again. 

Positives: rapport with that person I whiged to ... Possibly, although they could easily just be appeasing me.. Just because they are receptive and say they agree , doesn't mean they really agree. 
**Lesson learnt**: There are better ways to build rapport than whingeing.
