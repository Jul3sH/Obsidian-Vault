Situation: Didn't take advantage of grace period at BG to get out - leave before pushed.

Root Cause: False sense of security from getting lots of agency interest in my CV. I was using that as an excuse to procrastinate. Other people recognised the urgency and got the hell out first, and even offered to help me out. I also chose to ignore the reality that there will be numerous people trying for each postion. so it's not just about interest in my CV, it's about market conditions (which I also made assumptions on).

Lesson Learn't: Recognise that I am procrastinating in future, and don't tell myself stories. Also always be proactive, prepare ealrly, and leave before being pushed. It also probably surprised people that I wasn't doing anything. It looks weak. I was probably insecure in my ability, so I should have started revising and applying jobs to see what it was really about. Get's back to my **Affirmatins: I always get the facts, and never work on Assumptions**. - Well I didn't here.
