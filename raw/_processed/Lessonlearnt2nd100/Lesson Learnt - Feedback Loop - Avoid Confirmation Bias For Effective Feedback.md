****Situation****
\- I was convinced that I was right about the patio needing to be large enough to account for an extension, and was working out ways to convinve cloadgh, when in fact Mum pointed out that they can easily pick up the slabs and move them. 

**Root cause**
\- whist I was taking a step back and working out ways to convince Clodagh rather than ramming my opinion down her throat, what I didn't do was consider that I may be wrong, and think about both options with the same level of creativity. 

**Lesson's learned** - Make sure that I don't find myself in a position of defending a suggestion and not considering other peoples suggestions with an open mind. If I am wrong, and push forward with various tactics of persuasion, it could just mean that it back fires. 

Actually listen to other peoples opinions with an open mind. If they differ to my own, make sure I properly consider them by being mindful of confirmation bias.
