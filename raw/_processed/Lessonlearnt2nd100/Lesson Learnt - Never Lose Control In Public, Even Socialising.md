Situation: made a fantastic first impression at wedding. Then got too drunk, and possibly got a little carried away with personal Develoment preaching. 
Root cause: I may have been fine, but was too drunk to remember. 

Lesson learnt: if people see me turn into a drunken idiot, then they will question my control, will power etc, and I will also be not as good a judge at whether I am becoming too preachy or not. So 2 lessons, first is control the drinking. Second is make an impression, and then move on OR create a desire and only elaborate when asked.
