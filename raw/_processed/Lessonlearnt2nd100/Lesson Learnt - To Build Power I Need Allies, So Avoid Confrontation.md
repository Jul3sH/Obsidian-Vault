**Situation**: I was going to send a snotty email to Colin, he called 2 minutes later, we talked, and he was much more on side.
**Root cause**: I escaped sending an email that would have soured the relationship further. 
**Lesson learnt**:  I need allies to build a power base, so be assertive but explain why I have to take a certain stance, and avoid being confrontational. Keep enemies close.
