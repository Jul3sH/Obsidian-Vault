SIT: After a brief trial I fully committed to using a new task management system at BT, on a new job, under a lot pf pressure. It didn’t appear to be working, but I had fully committed to it and was far too busy to revert back.

RC:  It was not a goodtime to introduce change, but the other lesson here was that I should have piloted it for a while first. I made the full commitment because it was cool and new.

LL: Don’t full commit to shiny and new until I have tested it.
