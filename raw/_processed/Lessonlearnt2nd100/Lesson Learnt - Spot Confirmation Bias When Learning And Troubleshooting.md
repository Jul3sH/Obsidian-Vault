**Situation:** Lots of situations troubleshooting where I have missed the obvious, or reading technical documentation were I have got the wrong end of the stick, and come up with amazing theories to back something that is wrong. 

**Root cause:** I have an imaginative mind, and historically have been great at making up theories that sound good, and have previously gone off on a tangent based on these without establishing more facts. 
**Lesson Learned:** When reading documentation, especially technical or process stuff,  make sure to be asking myself….. could I be making assumptions here, OR have I possibly gone off on the wrong tangent. Could there be a much more simple explanation.
