Situation - for 6 months I have been ignoring the fact that every day I set myself 6 things to do ( it was a lot more), and achieve 1 or 2 of them.
Root cause: because I know I am unlikely to achieve them, I don't push myself to do so. 
Lesson learnt : reduce the number to 3, and follow the models if Most Important Tasks (3) and setting achievable goals (initially to get into the habit of achieving them).
