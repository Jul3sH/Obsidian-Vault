**Situation** **-** We had endless arguments over the DA standards with QGC, and produced confused standards at best. 

**Root cause** \- Because we weren't ultimatley clear on our objectives. 

**Lesson Learnt** \- We never got clear and accountable confirmation from the RNMS what our ultimate objective was with the standards. We should have created an action in the  the minutes, and left this in week after week as an audit trail until addressed properly. We should have had a crystal clear agenda, and minuted the meetings every week. If the standards were a box ticking exercise, then we should have found a creative way to document this so that we could get confirmation from the RNMs, as they would have continued to skirt around the commitment if uncomfortable. 

Create clear objectives, get these signed offer, list constraints, evaluate progress.
