**Situation:** Got wound up thinking that a message on Facebook from Andrew Ashendon to Matt was a reference to Clodagh fancying Matt. I eventully confronted him in a jokey manner and it was clearly something else

**Root cause:** Both Clodagh and I jumped to the same conclusion, and I took a while before I sepped back and thought, it could easily be something else, and if it is, I’ll end up falling out with him for making the accusation.

**Lesson leant**: I mulled over it, meditated, cooled down, thought to myself “nothing has actually happened here, so chill”, and confronted him making a joke about it. This was a classic letting my imagination run away with me, and thinking negatively. I should have applied leaned optimism at an earlier stage. What I did do correctly, was no to respond immediate, and run several increasingly laid back messages to myself before eventually responding.
