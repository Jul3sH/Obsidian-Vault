黎得基

**THIS IS THE WILL** of me **FELIX LESLIE HART** also known as **HART, FELIX LESLIE** the holder of Hong Kong Permanent Identity Card No.K299944(7) of 7, Capital Gardens, Ta Ku Ling San Tsuen, Sai Kung, New Territories, Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China.

### Revocation

1. I revoke all my previous wills and codicils.
    

### Declaration and Jurisdiction

2. I declare that: 2.1 I am domiciled in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China ("Hong Kong SAR"); 2.2 This will ("my Will") and any codicil to it must be construed and take effect according to the law of the Hong Kong SAR; 2.3 My Will and any codicil to it extends to all real and personal property of mine which is situated at my death in any part of the world.
    

### Appointment of Executors and Trustees

3.1 I appoint **LEE, SIU MAN WENDY (李少文)** the holder of Hong Kong Permanent Identity Card No.G520631(5) of House 7, Capital Gardens, Ta Ku Ling San Tsuen, Sai Kung, New Territories, Hong Kong SAR, to be the executor and trustee of my Will but if she predeceases me or is otherwise unable or unwilling to act then I appoint **JULIAN HART** of 48 Cecil Road, Wimbledon, London SW19 1JP, United Kingdom, the holder of United Kingdom Passport No.540536766 to be the executor and trustee of my Will, but if he predeceases me or is otherwise unable or unwilling to act then I appoint **HILARY ANN LESLIE TWIST** of Long Ridge House, 5 Wood Drive, St. Mellion Park, Cornwall, PL12 6UR, United Kingdom, to be the executor and trustee of my Will. 3.2 The expression "my Trustees" means the person or persons appointed under clause 3.1 of my Will or any other persons who act as my personal representative.

### Pecuniary Legacies

4. I give to my dear sister **HILARY ANN LESLIE TWIST** as mentioned above, the sum of Ten Thousand Pounds Sterling. This small amount is a totally inadequate reflection of the unconditional love and lifelong friendship she has given me for so long as I can remember as we have navigated the many twists of the paths of our respective lives.
    

### Gifts

5.1 I give my collection of watches, cufflinks and other items of personal adornment to my Trustees to permit the following persons to select one item each as mementos: (a) my sister **HILARY ANN LESLIE TWIST** as mentioned above; (b) **JOHN HENRY HALL** of 'Scallywag', 8 Wallingford Road, North Stoke, Wallingford, OX10 6BD, United Kingdom, my firm respected and loyal friend since our first uncertain days in the RAF together. 5.2 In giving effect to this provision my Trustees: 5.2.1 may in the event of any dispute decide the items they shall receive; 5.2.2 may permit more than one item to be selected if together they constitute a set; and 5.2.3 must ensure that any selection is made within a reasonable time of my death; 5.3 Any items that are not selected or allocated form part of the residue of my estate.

### Jointly-owned property

6. I give to my wife **LEE, SIU MAN WENDY (李少文)** as mentioned above all of my interest in any real or personal property which is owned by us in our joint names.
    

### Residue

7.1 I give the residue of my estate (out of which must be paid my debts, taxes and funeral and testamentary expenses) to my Trustees on trust to retain or sell it ("the Trust Fund"). 7.2 My Trustees must divide the Trust Fund into 2 equal shares and hold them as follows: 7.2.1 for my dear wife **LEE SIU MAN WENDY** absolutely, who has brought me such enormous and unexpected happiness and enriched my life and understanding since that fateful evening in the Clearwater Bay Golf Club in June 1995; 7.2.2 for my son **JULIAN HART** absolutely. 7.3 If my wife **LEE SIU MAN WENDY** dies before me then my son **JULIAN HART** takes by substitution the share of the Trust Fund that my wife **LEE SIU MAN WENDY** would have taken had she survived me. 7.4 If my son **JULIAN HART** dies before me leaving issue living at my death who attain 18 years then such issue take absolutely by substitution and if more than one in equal shares the share of the Trust Fund that my son **JULIAN HART** would have taken if he had survived me. 7.5 If my son **JULIAN HART** dies before me without leaving issue or leaves issue who do not at my death attain the age of 18 years then my wife **LEE SIU MAN WENDY** takes by substitution absolutely the share of the Trust Fund that my son **JULIAN HART** would have taken had he survived me. 7.6 If both of the shares is the Trust Fund mentioned in clauses 7.2.1 and 7.2.2 of my Will fail then my Trustees must hold the entire Trust Fund for my sister **HILARY ANN LESLIE TWIST**, as mentioned above, absolutely.

### Power of Investment

8. My Trustees may invest the Trust Fund in any investments they could make if they owned the Trust Fund personally. In particular my Trustees may: 8.1 invest in unsecured loans, including interest-free loans to a beneficiary; 8.2 invest in land, whether as an investment or as a residence for a beneficiary, and may improve and maintain it; 8.3 invest jointly with another person; 8.4 invest in wasting or non-income-producing assets; 8.5 invest anywhere in the world; and 8.6 choose not to diversify investments.
    

### Power of Advancement

9. My Trustees may apply the share of any beneficiary for his or her benefit and this power: 9.1 is in addition to any other powers my Trustees may have, in particular section 34 of the Trustee Ordinance, Chapter 29 of the Laws of the Hong Kong SAR; 9.2 applies to the whole or part of the beneficiary's share; 9.3 applies whether the beneficiary's right is vested, contingent, expectant or presumptive but not where another person is entitled in priority; 9.4 may be used for advancement, maintenance, education or any other benefit; 9.5 may be exercised by paying the share to a third party or, where appropriate, to the beneficiary direct.
    

### Power of Appropriation

10. My Trustees may give specific assets of the Trust Fund to beneficiaries without requiring consent (section 68 of the Probate and Administration Ordinance, Chapter 10 of the Laws of the Hong Kong SAR therefore applies subject to that amendment).
    

### Power to Insure

11. My Trustees may: 11.1 keep any assets of the Trust Fund insured against any risk and in any amount; 11.2 pay insurance premiums from the income or capital of that part of the Trust Fund which is held on the same trusts as the insured property; and 11.3 treat any insurance money received as though it were the proceeds of sale of the insured property.
    

### Power to Borrow

12.1 My Trustees may borrow money for any purpose. 12.2 My Trustees may charge any assets of my estate as security for any liability.

### Exclusion of Trustee's liability and Indemnity

13.1 In the absence of proof of dishonesty or the willful commission of an act known to be a breach of trust my Trustees shall not be liable for any loss. 13.2 My Trustees shall be entitled to be indemnified out of the assets of the Trust Fund against all liabilities incurred in connection with the bona fide execution of their duties and exercise of their powers.

### Power to use nominees

14. My Trustees may vest any asset of the Trust Fund in the name of, or under the control of: 14.1 one or more of their number; or 14.2 any person (which may include a trust cooperation) or people as nominee(s).
    

### Power to charge for professional services

15. Any trustee of my Will who is engaged in a profession or business may charge fees for work done by him or her or by his or her firm in connection with the trusts of my Will including acts which an executor or trustee not being in any profession or business could have done personally.
    

### Minor's receipt

16. My Trustees may pay or transfer to the parent or guardian of any minor or to any minor over the age of 16 years any sum of money being income of the Trust Fund intended to be applied for the maintenance, education or benefit of that minor or any sum of money or other capital of the Trust Fund which it is intended should be applied for the advancement or benefit of that minor upon receiving from any such parent, guardian or minor over the age of 16 years an undertaking in writing to apply such sums of money or other property in the manner intended and the receipt of any such parent, guardian or minor over 16 years shall be a complete discharge to my Trustee.
    

### Apportionment

17. My Trustees may decide whether or not the Apportionment Ordinance, Chapter 18 of the Laws of the Hong Kong SAR, is applied to the Trust Fund.
    

### Use of powers

18. My Trustees may exercise any of their powers: 18.1 from time to time; and 18.2 in their absolute discretion.
    

### Power of Administer outside the Hong Kong SAR

19. My Trustees may administer the trusts of my estate outside the Hong Kong SAR.
    

### Commorientes

20. My estate is to be divided as if any person who dies within 28 days of my death had predeceased me.
    

### Definition of "Issue"

21. In my Will the term "issue": 21.1 includes those who are legitimate or adopted; but 21.2 does not include those who are illegitimate or step-children, or natural children that have been adopted by another person.
    
22. I declare that issue conceived before my death and born living thereafter must for the purposes of my Will be deemed to have been living at my death.
    

### Funeral Wishes

23. I wish that my body be cremated and that my funeral service be conducted according to the rites of the Church of England.

In witness of which I have signed my name on the Fourth day of November 2011.
