Situation is when I'm given time to do something, I piss around for ages doing it from the bottom up, and then I suddenly panic and run out of time. Very efficiently, just start working on what I needed to do in the first place, which is the deliverable, and do it from the top down, having wasted a ton of time pontificating and overthinking everything.
The root cause is again perhaps linked to my ADHD. Until I really realize I'm screwed, I don't jump into action, and then the chemicals are released in my brain that get me to focus on the right thing. Until that point, I'm just researching and planning another ADHD-type things.
I think the lesson here, other than finding ways to trick myself into the urgency, is to just start with the indeliverable.
