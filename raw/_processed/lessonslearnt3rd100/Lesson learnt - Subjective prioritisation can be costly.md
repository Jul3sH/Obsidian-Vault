> **Situation:** I messed an opportunity to apply for a well aligned Solution Lead role in NTT because I was prioritising prep for TTI. In reality the Prep I did was overkill and may never be needed, however, I did it because it's also a fundamental learning for cyber security I felt I needed and I like studying.
> **Root cause:** I was convinced TTI was going to happen and also felt that I need to apply for NTT through a contact and procrastinated on which contact to use.
> **Lesson Learnt:** I need to challenge myself constantly on my priorities to ensure they are objective rather than subjective. This was procrastination and an cloaked distraction from my primary objective to get a job.
> Part of the problem is that I am still a little in denial that I actually need a job urgently. I need to look a my finances and be clear on the daily financial impact of not having a role to create a strong need.
