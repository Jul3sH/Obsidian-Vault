**Situation:** CIO Raja asked me a question and I answered a slightly different one. It was because I didn't quite get the question, so rather than question it and say "just so I'm clear", I answered what I thought it was. 
**Root Cause:** I was probably playing tapes rather than catching the beginning of the sentence.
**Lesson Learnt:** listen, if I didn't get the question, ask. It's not embarrassing because I didn't get the question, it shows I am a listener and I am answering the query. It's annoying when people don't answer the question.
