**Situation**: I kept starting to talk before David Crawford had finished his sentence 
**Root Cause**: Even though I thought David had finished, he was just pausing. I was too keen to respond, which meant I had probably decided what I was going to say early rather than listening to everything he had to say. 
**Lessons learnt:** Make sure I listen to EVERYTHING that someone is saying
