**Situation:**  In the ILP class Harshill got really upset when he realised ’no one cares” about him going on probation, and I realised that I make a big drama about things to demonstrate how tough life is for me…… but no one cares. In fact all that happens is they get stressed and/or angry if their stories start to think I’m blaming them. 
**Root cause:** No one cares, and the universe certainly doesn’t care. Colin pointed out the exercise of telling the universe all of your reasons for not ding things until it becomes clear that it doesn’t care, and he also used the lost keys example also to point out that no one cares about the drama you get into when you lose your keys. 
**Lesson learnt:** People and the universe don't care about your lost keys and/or your reasons for not doing things.
