**Situation:** Gave up on ILP telling myself “I could succeed if I tried but it’s not a priority for me”. I then used my “i’m to busy doing important things” trump card to get Colin off my back, and focussed on valuer goals. 

Thinking to myself “fuck this deal” I’m leaving anyway when it was going tits up. 
Many other examples. Thinking I hate work after failing as SOLA, when in fact I love it when I am confident and know my shit. 

**Root cause:** I liked sitting in the space of “if i try i could get the results but I cannot be bothered, and I use this to avoid trying hard and failing. I hate being confronted with the prospect of not being good enough and give up as soon as it gets tuff.

**Lesson learnt:** Spot self sabotage to avoid failure. I need to remind myself what the outcome is, remind myself of this trait and not give up s easily when it gets tuff.
