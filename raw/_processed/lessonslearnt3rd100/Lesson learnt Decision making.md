Originally wanted 2400 rent, it’s a renters market
After 2 weeks of unsuccessful viewing reduced to
2350\. Offer made for 2200, I countered with 2250

The agent  immediately said that should be very achievable, and they accepted within minutes

I probably could have said 23000 and got it easily, so I will lose 1,200 over 2 years

**Root cause: Emotional decision on the spot.**
I was busy and didn’t want to risk losing them with just a month left, and Jamie had just confirmed the timeframe was perfect for him to renovate the apartment. So it was a bit of panick combined with feeling discomfort asking for more and just wanting it done.

**What should I have done**
Gone online to check what else is on the market so I had a baseline to work with, recognised my discomfort was getting in the way of doing the right thing, and initially put in a higher offer.
