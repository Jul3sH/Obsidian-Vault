**Situation:** when I schedule work with others, whether it's in the past, it's been as members of my team, or when I get dragged into work with other people, I'm extremely efficient. When I don't, I can get caught up in cloaked distraction and procrastination such as endless research loops. There are many examples of this throughout my career and personal life.
**Root cause:** It's an ADHD trait, I enjoy research and learning, I find getting started dull. I focused on researching a topic; I go off on a massive tangent. Once i get started however, my hyper focus moves on to achieving the goals.
**Lesson learnt:** Commit to doing stuff with people to get started and move my focus to where it needs to be.
