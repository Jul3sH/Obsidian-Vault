**Situation:** I was going to move the CAP course at the last minute because I wanted to watch to rugby. I came up with loads of reasons like it might be the last chance with my Dad, but ultimately I just fancied watching rugby more 
**Root cause:** I don’t have any integrity when it comes to myself. This is why i can’t crate new habits, allow distractions to get in the way of things I have committed to achieving, and don’t achieve outcomes.
**Lesson learnt:** I must treat every commitment I make to myself, however small, like my life depends on it. I must also not make commitments to myself if I do not intend on keeping them with this level of integrity
