**Situation:** Focussing on development of rather than getting a job, when as soon as I got a job prospect from HSBC I was confident I could get, my focus immediately swapped with conviction to getting a job
**Root cause:** It seems this is linked to my non-agreement “i’m not ready, I’m not prepared enough, what’s the point’ type thinking, which is ultimately linked to my fear of failure. It seems I just don’t want to get started or try if I don’t think i’m going to be any good
**Lesson learnt:** I must spot convincing distractions that are linked to me avoiding priorities because of a fear of failure
