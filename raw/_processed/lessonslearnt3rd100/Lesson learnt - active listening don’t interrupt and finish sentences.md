**Situation:** Rob kept trying to make a point that I was not listening to what the problem is. 
**Root Cause:** Actually I did, and actually I was right but he has a massive ego and doesn't want to be questioned. 
**Lessons Learnt:** Listen intently. If I interrupt, anticipate what people are saying, or resond before people have finished ( or importantly, look like I have drawn my conclusion before they have finished ) it gets their backs up, and they look for reason to try to prove I didn't listen properly, like suggesting that I haven't solved the problem they are talking about. So listen with intent, and don't do any of the above.
