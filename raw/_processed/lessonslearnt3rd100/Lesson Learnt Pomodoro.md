The situation is that I've recently noticed my brain's urges to pick up my phone after I've done a significant piece of thinking. Not even a lot, not like 20 minutes, just a little bit to pick up my phone.
I think the root cause is probably a little bit of its ADHD, because I know this. I remember this from when I used to be in my programming days, when I was very young, sitting in Marina Cove and there wasn't a phone, but I would just sort of get up and do something else.
So the lesson learned is that I just need to resist it. I need to have the phone out of sight and find something else for those two minutes, because it's quite easy to come back to if I resist the urge for a minute. The Pomodoro was also working quite well because I tell myself that I can have that distraction every 30 minutes.
