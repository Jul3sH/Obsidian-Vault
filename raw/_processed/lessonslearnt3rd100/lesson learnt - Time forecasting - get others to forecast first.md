Important considerations - 

People will accept I am saying it will take longer if I justify it.
Moving deadlines all the time once I have set expectations, as I did at CC, looks much worse than saying" it's more efficient to get it right first time, I will need to add some tolerance as there are so many assumptions at this stage" 
You have to do seven things well to account for one thing done badly. 
People naturally under estimate.

Remember that it was one of the biggest tips from Mark when joining CC. 

If I am challenged, throw the ball in their court by making them forecast, and then I can sub telly challenge them back ;-) 

Ask, is that outside of your expectations ?
What is your expectation ?
Why xxx days, can I ask what you are basing this on ? 
I will then get a feel for whether they are just hopefully, desperate, chancing it, passing on their problem, or have genuine cause to believe it should take less. 

If it's previous this or that, as it's likely to be, ask specifically what similarities the projects had... And then pull them apart based on assumptions
