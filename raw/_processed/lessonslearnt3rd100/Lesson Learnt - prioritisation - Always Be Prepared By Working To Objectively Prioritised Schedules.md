**Situation** **\-** unimpressive meeting with the consultancy firm and Stuart, which ultimately may have given him a long term negative impression of my inter personal skills and ability. 

**Root cause** - I didn't have much to contribute, because I din't know much about the project, and hadn't clarified prior to the meeting specifically what Stuart was looking for from my attendance. I was busy, so had done a bit of background reading. 
**Lesson Learnt** \- I should always be prepared for meetings in which I have an opportunity to shine, regardless of how busy I am, this was an absolute priority, and probably a test which ultimately I didn't impress in. I should have approached Gio in a clever way and said "you are great in meetings with Stuart, he really lets you take lead", and then listened for tips (direct approach would have got me very little no doubt!).
