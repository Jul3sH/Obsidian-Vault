****Situation:**** Manager taking Credit for my resolution to issues. **Root Cause;** because he can, and does. **Lesson Learned.** When I made him feel like we were reolving the issues together, and kept reinforcing the fact we had done that, he seemed more responsive. He's not going to plug me instead of himself, so don't go there, but he may plug us a team that work well together, as it makes him look like a team player, who encourages collaboration.
