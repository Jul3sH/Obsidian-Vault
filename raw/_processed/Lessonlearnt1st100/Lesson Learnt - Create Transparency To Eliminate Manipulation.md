****Situation:**** Being held accountable for deliverables not being achieved,  and allowing the RMs to distort the situation when reporting to Stuart.

**Root Cause:** We weren't getting visible by-in from the RM, and they were then able to distort the facts to SM. 

**Lesson Learned.** Sell the idea to my immediate report, i.e the RMs, and then get visible agreement. It is then THEIR responsibility to communicate this to the director. As long as I have proff they had by in, then they can't push the blame onto us.
