Sit: When interviewing I was helping the interviewee out too much. We need to see how they react  under pressure. In another interview I was also too in awe of a candidate during interveiwing, and gave him an easy ride because I was embarrassed to ask someone with that much experience fundamental routing questions. 
LL: If he's good, don't be embarrassed to ask technical questions, just start with a disclaimer such as " Ok, so whilst these questions may seem little silly, we are required to run through them as a formality. People are great at talking about what they know, so you must put them on the spot. PMs have actress understanding of technical considerations and constraints, but you wouldn't let them design a network.
