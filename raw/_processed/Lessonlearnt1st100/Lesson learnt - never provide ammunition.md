I must change my belief system, never tell people things that could compromise me, it can come back to bite me. Especially at work, you never know who will use it against  you, but I experienced it before where people when they have their backs against the wall change.

Trigger – feeling the urge to be a lad and impress

Tool – snap into diplomatic mode, insinuate if I really feel the need and have already caught myself half way through, but stop short of saying anything that anyone could use against me. I have already established that means talking negatively about other people. It achieves very little. It makes you look like a back stabber, and can be used against you.
