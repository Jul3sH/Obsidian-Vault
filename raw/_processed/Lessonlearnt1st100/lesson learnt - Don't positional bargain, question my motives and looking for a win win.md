Situation: Clodagh wanted to drop off bags at hotel the night before for Marathon. We ended up rowing. 
Root cause: It annoyed me because it was only a 20 minute detour on the way, and she was being precious about her sleep. Clodagh was obviously worried about being tired and menstrual. I needed my phone to meet my friends, which she didn't seem to bothered about. We ended up positional bargaining. 
Lesson learnt. I went straight into the creative process of thinking of reasons why it had to be my way. Actually by focussing that creative energy on making her suggestion work, I realised it also was ok, and we have a win win. I need to get out of the habit of using my creativity to back up a position, and use it to create win win. That will get me further in life,
