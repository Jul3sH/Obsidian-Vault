**Situation:** Turned around discussion with vodafone
**Root cause:** I went in with the attitude of being fair, helpful, and looking for resolution.

When I asked the right questions, we established that their hands were tied due to commercial governance. 

If we'd understood the governance position early on maybe we could have saved ourselves a lot of iterations 

Feeling I got was that this is a a first for them too.

Maybe we could engage them earlier in the process 

**Lesson learnt:** I built rapport and respect by answering intelligently when challenged, and focus on the beniifts to them. As I was also clearly looking for ways to give them what they want, this also meant that he was fair and listened. 

Gained customer confidence being solution oriented
