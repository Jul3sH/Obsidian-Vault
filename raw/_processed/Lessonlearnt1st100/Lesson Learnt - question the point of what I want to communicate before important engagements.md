Situation: At Consultancy day I approached the director of Consulting to introduce myself. Whilst I was bulish and personable, I wasn't crystical clear with my feedback on what I thought of the day, it could have been a lot clearer and specific.
Root cause: I thought, grab your chance, just go for it.
Lesson Learnt: Before any engagement, however brief, I need to think "what is the point of this engagement", gather my thoughts, and make sure I understand my purpose so that the interaction has purpose. I also need to have a polished elevator pitch.
