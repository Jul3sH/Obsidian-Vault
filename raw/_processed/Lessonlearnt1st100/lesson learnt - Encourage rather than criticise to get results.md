Situation:  I have been getting annoyed at clodagh for not doing anyting outside work, and it has been causing rows, and she still wasn't doing anything. 
Root cause: She mentioned when upset something about me thinking she is lazy and useless, and was clearly upset. She also clearly doesn't like being told how to go about it, partly becasue of my tone. So maybe the point missed is that she correlates it to negative aspects of her work environment, like being micro managed belittled. 

lesson learnt: Set objectives, back off, and ask if I can help or something more subtle, and how are we doing, with a positive tone. Allow her to work out that it's obvious she's slipped on deadlines or ibjectives. Make sure I don't Micro mange. Use this as program management practice. Set a PID with financial goals and tolerances.
