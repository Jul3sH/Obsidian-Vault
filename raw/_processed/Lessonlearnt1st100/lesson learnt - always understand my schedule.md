Sit: I couldn’t advise Vickie of my deadline to review the riverbed design

RC: My NTP had slipped and I hadn’t established  and agreed a new deadline. This was because I was concerned that I may not have it completed by the end of week so I was avoiding the conversation, so procrastination and avoidance of confrontation were the two RC.

LL: As soon as something slips assess the impact and adjust my schedule to reflect the impact. Then immediately set new expectations. Don’t procrastinate on having the difficult conversation, if it comes up in a reactive manner instead of proactive it’s always both harder to recover, and looks bad.  Also don’t procrastinate because I have to make tough decisions.

I also need to be aware of all deadlines so I am more likely to really manage distractions, and be assertive. As soon as I notice myself thinking “I don’t really have to meet this deadline” think to myself “oh yes I do”
