Sit: Stressing out worrying, trying to get rid of the anxiety by meditating and convincing myself it’s probably nothing to worry about. What eventually did stop me stressing immediately, was creating a task to confront the fear immediately (next possible opportunity).

RC: I can’t lie to my myself  and I don’t enjoy uncertainty (fear), so it doesn’t go away unless I deal with it. Coming up with a plan to deal with it immediately sorts my head out.

LL: Eliminate fear by dealing with it immediately  rather than sit around worrying about it. Even if the activity to eliminate the fear creates more  fear, it will be temporary and shorter lived. Constantly worrying is time consuming. Having a plan of action to deal with the root cause helps alleviate  the fear.
