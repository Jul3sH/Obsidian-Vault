Situation:  CC - everyone was ok with me leaving, and had read between the lines. I felt the need to point out that Dad wasn't terminally ill. 
Root cause: I said it to get it off my chest because I felt guilty. There was no other reason as the situation didn't need improving. 
Lesson Learnt: I should have said nothing, there was no need, so all that could happen as a result is for things to get worse. Refrain from needing to get things off my chest, it's weak and is unnecessary.
