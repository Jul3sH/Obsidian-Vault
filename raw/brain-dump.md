# Brain Dump  -  Capture Inbox

Zero-evaluation capture. Raw, unclassified ideas, links, and notes  -  things to go
through later. Nothing here is committed to or even assessed. The only job of this
file is to silence the "I'll lose this idea" anxiety so it stops pulling focus
(see [[prioritization-framework]]).

**This is the inbox that sits *before* the whole planning hierarchy. It is NOT a
list of projects.** Items here might become a project, a BAU task, reference
knowledge, or nothing at all  -  that's decided later, not now.

During a review/compile pass, each item is triaged to exactly one destination  -  or binned:

| If the item is… | Route it to |
|-----------------|-------------|
| A project-sized initiative (≥5h, with a payoff worth a hypothesis) | [[funnel]]  -  add a hypothesis + trigger; gets a POR card |
| A standalone task/deliverable idea (XXS, <5h) | [[funnel]]  -  add as XXS candidate; no POR card needed until promoted |
| Reference knowledge or learning to absorb | Compile into the wiki |
| Going nowhere | Delete it |

**Why it lives in `raw/`:** it's the same capture-then-process inbox already used
for dropped files  -  just persistent. Unlike one-shot raw files, this file is **not**
moved to `raw/_processed/`; only its individual items get routed out.

The **Note** column is an optional one-line reminder of why the item was captured  - 
not an evaluation. No hypothesis, size, or score is required to drop something here.
